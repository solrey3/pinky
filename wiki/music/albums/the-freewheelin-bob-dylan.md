# Bob Dylan — The Freewheelin’ Bob Dylan

**Release Date:** 1963-05-27  
**Wikipedia:** [The Freewheelin’ Bob Dylan](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Freewheelin'_Bob_Dylan)

## Overview

The Freewheelin' Bob Dylan is the second studio album by the American singer-songwriter Bob Dylan. It was released on May 27, 1963, through Columbia Records. The record marks the beginning of Dylan's writing contemporary lyrics to traditional melodies. His debut album Bob Dylan contains only two original songs, whereas eleven of the thirteen songs on Freewheelin' are Dylan's compositions.
