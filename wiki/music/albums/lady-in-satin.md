# Billie Holiday — Lady In Satin

**Release Date:** 1958-06  
**Wikipedia:** [Lady In Satin](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Lady_in_Satin)

## Overview

Lady in Satin is a studio album by American jazz singer Billie Holiday, released in 1958 on Columbia Records, catalogue CL 1157 in mono and CS 8048 in stereo. It is the penultimate album completed by the singer, and the last to be released in her lifetime. Her final album, Last Recording, was recorded in March 1959, and released just after her death. The original album was produced by Irving Townsend and engineered by Fred Plaut.
