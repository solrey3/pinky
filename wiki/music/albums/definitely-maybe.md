# Oasis — Definitely Maybe

**Release Date:** 1994-08-29  
**Label:** Creation Records  
**Producers:** Noel Gallagher, Mark Coyle, Owen Morris  
**Recorded:** January–April 1994 at Monnow Valley Studio, Sawmills Studios, Johnny Marr's studio  
**Wikipedia:** [Definitely Maybe](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Definitely_Maybe)

## Overview

Definitely Maybe is the debut studio album by the English rock band Oasis, released on 29 August 1994 by Creation Records. The album features Noel Gallagher on lead guitar, backing vocals and as chief songwriter, Liam Gallagher on lead vocals.

Definitely Maybe was an immediate commercial success in the United Kingdom. It went straight to number one in the UK Albums Chart and became the fastest-selling debut album in British music history at the time. It went on to be certified 10× platinum by the BPI. The album went on to sell over 15 million copies worldwide.

Upon release, Definitely Maybe received widespread critical acclaim and helped to spur a revitalisation in British pop/rock music in the 1990s. It is regarded as a cornerstone of the Britpop genre. In 2006, the NME conducted a readers' poll in which Definitely Maybe was voted the greatest album ever. Rolling Stone ranked the album at number 217 on its 2020 list of the 500 Greatest Albums of All Time.
