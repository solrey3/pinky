# Soundtrack — The Harder They Come

**Release Date:** 1972 (UK); February 1973 (North America)  
**Label:** Island Records (UK) / Mango Records (North America)  
**Producers:** Various  
**Wikipedia:** [The Harder They Come (soundtrack)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/The_Harder_They_Come_(soundtrack))

## Overview

The Harder They Come is the soundtrack album to the film of the same name, released in 1972 in the United Kingdom as Island Records ILPS 9202. It was issued in February 1973 in North America as Mango Records SMAS-7400. It peaked at No. 140 on the US Billboard 200.

The soundtrack features music by Jimmy Cliff, who stars in the film, as well as other Jamaican artists including Desmond Dekker, Toots and the Maytals, and The Melodians. The album played a major role in introducing reggae music to international audiences, particularly in the United States. It has been called "the album that took reggae worldwide."

In 2021, the album was deemed "culturally, historically, or aesthetically significant" by the Library of Congress and selected for preservation in the National Recording Registry.
