# The Smashing Pumpkins — Siamese Dream

**Release Date:** 1993-07-27  
**Label:** Virgin Records  
**Producers:** Butch Vig, Billy Corgan  
**Recorded:** 1992–1993 at Triclops Sound Studios, Marietta, Georgia  
**Wikipedia:** [Siamese Dream](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Siamese_Dream)

## Overview

Siamese Dream is the second studio album by the American alternative rock band the Smashing Pumpkins, released on July 27, 1993, by Virgin Records. The album was produced by Butch Vig and frontman Billy Corgan. Despite its recording sessions being fraught with difficulties and tensions, Siamese Dream debuted at number ten on the Billboard 200, and was eventually certified 4× Platinum by the RIAA, with the album selling over six million copies worldwide.

Four singles were released: "Cherub Rock", "Today", "Disarm", and "Rocket". The album received widespread acclaim from critics and audiences alike, with its diverse musical influences and lyrical material considered unique compared to other releases during the alternative rock and grunge movements of its time. The album has since been considered "one of the finest alternative rock albums", and is widely regarded as one of the greatest albums of the 1990s and of all time.
