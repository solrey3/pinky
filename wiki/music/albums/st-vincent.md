# St. Vincent — St. Vincent

**Release Date:** 2014-02-24  
**Label:** Loma Vista Recordings / Republic Records  
**Producers:** John Congleton  
**Recorded:** Elmwood studio, Dallas  
**Wikipedia:** [St. Vincent (album)](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/St._Vincent_(album))

## Overview

St. Vincent is the fourth studio album by American musician St. Vincent. It was released on February 24, 2014. Produced by John Congleton, it features collaborations with Sharon Jones & the Dap-Kings drummer Homer Steinweiss and Midlake drummer McKenzie Smith.

Critically acclaimed on its release, the album won a 2015 Grammy Award for Best Alternative Music Album, making St. Vincent only the second female solo artist to win the award since its inception in 1991. It peaked at No. 12 in the Billboard 200 and at No. 21 in the UK Albums Chart.
