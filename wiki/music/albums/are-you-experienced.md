# The Jimi Hendrix Experience — Are You Experienced

**Release Date:** 1967-05-12 (UK); 1967-08-23 (US)  
**Label:** Track Records (UK) / Reprise Records (US)  
**Producers:** Chas Chandler  
**Recorded:** October 1966 – April 1967 at De Lane Lea, CBS, and Olympic Studios, London  
**Wikipedia:** [Are You Experienced](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Are_You_Experienced)

## Overview

Are You Experienced is the debut studio album by the Jimi Hendrix Experience, released in May 1967. The album was an immediate critical and commercial success, and is widely regarded as one of the greatest albums of all time. It features Jimi Hendrix's innovative approach to songwriting and electric guitar playing, which soon established a new direction in psychedelic and rock music as a whole.

After struggling to earn a living on the R&B circuit as a backing guitarist, Hendrix signed a management and production contract in 1966 with former Animals bassist Chas Chandler. Chandler brought Hendrix to London and recruited members for the Jimi Hendrix Experience. Are You Experienced and its preceding singles were recorded over a five-month period from late October 1966 through early April 1967.

Released in the UK on May 12, 1967, Are You Experienced spent 33 weeks on the British charts, peaking at number two. The US version reached number five on the Billboard Top LPs chart, remaining on the chart for 106 weeks. Rolling Stone ranked Are You Experienced 30th on its 2020 list of the "500 Greatest Albums of All Time". In 2005, the album was added to the National Recording Registry.
