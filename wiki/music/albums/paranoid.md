# Black Sabbath — Paranoid

**Release Date:** 1970-09-18  
**Wikipedia:** [Paranoid](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Paranoid_(album))

## Overview

Paranoid is the second studio album by the British heavy metal band Black Sabbath. It was released on 18 September 1970 in the United Kingdom by Vertigo Records, and on 7 January 1971 in the United States by Warner Bros. Records. The album contains several of the band's signature songs, including "Iron Man", "War Pigs" and the title track, which was the band's only top 20 hit, reaching number four on the UK Singles Chart.
