# Salvador de Madariaga

Spanish diplomat, writer, historian, and critic. Best known in literary circles for his 1926 *Guía del lector del Quijote* (Reader's Guide to Don Quixote), which introduced the influential formulation of the mutual transformation between Don Quixote and Sancho Panza.

**Lived:** 1886–1978  
**Notable Work:** *Guía del lector del Quijote* (1926)
