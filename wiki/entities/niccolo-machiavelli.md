# Niccolò Machiavelli

Florentine diplomat, political philosopher, and author of *The Prince* (1513). His name became synonymous with cunning, pragmatic statecraft — a tradition he himself would likely have rejected as reductive. Imprisoned and tortured by the Medici in 1513, he wrote *The Prince* while in exile on his farm, hoping to regain political favor. He is the foundational figure of modern political realism.
