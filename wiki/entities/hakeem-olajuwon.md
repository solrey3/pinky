# Hakeem Olajuwon

The greatest center in the modern era and the most technically skilled big man ever.

**Born:** 1963, Lagos, Nigeria
**Nationality:** Nigerian-American
**Position:** C
**Active:** 1984–2002
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 3 (#9)

## Summary

The greatest center in the modern era and the most technically skilled big man ever. Hakeem's Dream Shake — a footwork-based post move so complex that it was studied like a dance — produced baskets against the greatest defenders who ever played. Two championships with the Houston Rockets (1994, 1995), including beating Patrick Ewing's Knicks and then Shaq's Magic in consecutive years.

## Highlights

- 2x NBA Champion (1994, 1995); 2x Finals MVP
- NBA MVP 1994; Defensive Player of the Year 1987, 1993
- The Dream Shake is the most technically complete post move in basketball history
- He was the only player to win MVP, Finals MVP, and Defensive Player of the Year in the same decade
- His 1995 playoff performance (beating the 60-win Spurs in 6 games, then sweeping the Magic) is the greatest playoff run of the 1990s
