# Allen Iverson

The Answer.

**Born:** 1975, Hampton VA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG/SG
**Active:** 1996–2010
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The Answer. One MVP (2001), one Finals appearance (with the Sixers against the Lakers), eleven All-Star selections. Iverson's crossing over Michael Jordan in Game 1 of the 2001 Finals, and his subsequent step-over of Jordan, is the most audacious individual playoff moment of his era. His 'Practice? We talking about practice!' press conference is the most quoted moment in sports media history.

## Highlights

- 1x NBA MVP (2001); 11x All-Star; 4x scoring champion
- The Jordan crossover (2001 Finals Game 1) is the single most audacious play in Finals history
- Practice. We talking about practice. Not a game. Practice.
- His 2001 Sixers run (with no legitimate second option) to the Finals is the greatest individual-carrying performance in Finals history
- He made wearing cornrows, arm sleeves, and tattoos mainstream — the most culturally influential player since Jordan
