# Swerve Strickland

#36.

**Real Name:** Trevin Adams
**Born:** 1991, Lynwood CA
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2013–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #36 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#36. The most technically diverse and athletically gifted young main event star in AEW. His feud with Hangman Adam Page (2023) produced AEW's best match of that year. His AEW World Championship win (2024) was built on two years of consistent excellence and one of the best entrance themes in wrestling. The 'Swerve's House' character development showed his range beyond pure athleticism.

## Highlights

- AEW World Champion (2024)
- His matches with Hangman Adam Page are AEW's best feud of 2023
- The Swerve Stomp (double stomp from the top rope to the floor) is wrestling's most dangerous-looking spot
- His NXT run (as Isaiah 'Swerve' Scott) demonstrated that WWE developmental was holding him back
- His feud with Prince Nana as his manager elevated the character beyond pure wrestling
