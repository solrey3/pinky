# Wilco

American alternative rock band formed in Chicago in 1994. Led by Jeff Tweedy, the band evolved from the ashes of Uncle Tupelo.

## Albums

- [Yankee Hotel Foxtrot (2002)](../music/albums/yankee-hotel-foxtrot.md) — Fourth studio album; widely regarded as one of the greatest albums of the 2000s. Ranked #33 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
