# Bill Simmons

American sports writer, podcaster, and founder of The Ringer. Simmons built his career at ESPN before launching his own media company. Known for blending statistical analysis with pop-culture references, storytelling, and deeply opinionated rankings, he has been one of the most influential voices in basketball analysis since the early 2000s.
