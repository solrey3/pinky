# Jon Moxley / Dean Ambrose

#18.

**Real Name:** Jonathan Good
**Born:** 1985, Cincinnati OH
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2004–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #18 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#18. The most credible 'dangerous man' character in modern wrestling. As Dean Ambrose in WWE and Jon Moxley in AEW, his unhinged intensity reads as genuine rather than performed. AEW World Champion multiple times; his unsanctioned matches and death match work have built a reputation as the most violently committed performer of his generation. His IWGP title run (2020) added international credibility.

## Highlights

- Multiple AEW World Champion; WWE IC Champion (as Ambrose)
- His unsanctioned matches (with Seth Rollins, Kenny Omega) are the best brawling of the last decade
- His NJPW run legitimized AEW's cross-promotional aspirations
- The Blackpool Combat Club faction (with Claudio and Wheeler Yuta) is built around his legitimate physical credibility
- His promos are the most genuinely menacing in wrestling — no catchphrases, all threat
