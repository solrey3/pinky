# John Stockton

The all-time assists leader (15,806) and all-time steals leader (3,265) by enormous margins.

**Born:** 1962, Spokane WA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 1984–2003
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 5 (#28)

## Summary

The all-time assists leader (15,806) and all-time steals leader (3,265) by enormous margins. Stockton played his entire 19-year career in Utah with Karl Malone — the greatest pick-and-roll partnership in basketball history. Never won a championship; went to the Finals twice (1997, 1998) and lost to Jordan's Bulls both times.

## Highlights

- All-time NBA assists leader (15,806 — 3,000 more than second place)
- All-time NBA steals leader (3,265)
- 19x All-Star; 10x All-NBA; 5x All-Defensive
- His pick-and-roll with Karl Malone is the most productive offensive partnership in NBA history (by assists/points produced)
- His 1997 Western Conference Finals buzzer-beater three against Houston is one of the most celebrated shots in NBA history
