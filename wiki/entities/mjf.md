# MJF

#21.

**Real Name:** Maxwell Jacob Friedman
**Born:** 1997, Rye NY
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2015–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #21 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#21. The greatest heel character in wrestling since Ric Flair. MJF's promo ability is generationally rare — he was delivering ROH-quality character work at 21. His AEW World Championship reigns have produced some of the best long-term storylines in AEW's history, including the Wardlow power struggle and the CM Punk rivalry. The MJF/Punk cage match at Double or Nothing 2022 is a masterwork.

## Highlights

- AEW World Champion (multiple reigns)
- His ROH debut promo at 21 immediately established him as a generational heel talent
- The MJF/Wardlow storyline (servant turns on master) is one of the best-executed builds in AEW
- MJF vs. CM Punk cage match (Double or Nothing 2022) is among the best matches of the 2020s
- His Salt of the Earth Pillman-lock submission adds technical credibility to the character
