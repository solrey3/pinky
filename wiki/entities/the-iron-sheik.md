# The Iron Sheik

#79.

**Real Name:** Hossein Khosrow Vaziri
**Born:** 1942, Isfahan, Iran
**Nationality:** Iranian-American
**Active:** 1972–1988
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #79 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#79. The greatest foreign heel in wrestling history. The Iron Sheik's title win over Bob Backlund (1983) and subsequent loss to Hulk Hogan (January 1984) launched WrestleMania's commercial possibility. A legitimate Iranian amateur wrestling champion and Olympic wrestling coach, his character — anti-American, pro-Iranian revolutionary — arrived at the perfect cultural moment of anti-Iranian sentiment to generate maximum heat.

## Highlights

- WWF World Heavyweight Champion (brief, historic reign)
- His title win over Bob Backlund and subsequent loss to Hogan created the commercial foundation for WrestleMania
- His legitimate Iranian amateur wrestling credentials gave the character genuine physical credibility
- The Camel Clutch is the most culturally loaded submission hold in wrestling history
- His later social media presence (late career) was genuinely funny — especially his feuds with various celebrities
