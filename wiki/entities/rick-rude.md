# Rick Rude

#61.

**Real Name:** Richard Rood
**Born:** 1958, Robbinsdale MN
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1983–1994
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #61 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#61. The most credibly arrogant character in wrestling. Rick Rude's physique-based heel gimmick — 'What I'd like to have right now is for all you fat, out-of-shape, Rubenesque pigs out there to keep the noise down while I take my robe off and show you what a real man looks like' — was the perfect foil for the Hulkamania babyface era. His matches with Warrior and Steamboat were consistently underrated.

## Highlights

- NWA/WCW US Champion; WWF IC Champion
- His feud with Jake 'The Snake' Roberts (1991) produced some of the best storytelling of that era
- His WCW television work (1992–1993) was among the best during that period
- The Rude Awakening (inverted neckbreaker) was the most elegant finishing move of his era
- His death in 1999 at 40 from heart failure robbed wrestling of a performer who would have had a great 2000s career
