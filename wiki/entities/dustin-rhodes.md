# Dustin Rhodes

#70.

**Real Name:** Dustin Rhodes
**Born:** 1969, Austin TX
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1988–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #70 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#70. The most committed character performer in wrestling history. The Goldust character — androgynous, methodical, psychologically manipulative — was unlike anything wrestling had done and was executed with total commitment for 25+ years. His biological relationship with Cody Rhodes gives the Rhodes family a complete three-generation story (Dusty, Dustin, Cody).

## Highlights

- Multiple IC and Tag Championship reigns
- The Goldust character (debuted 1995) was the most genuinely transgressive character in mainstream wrestling
- His hardcore match with Steve Austin at WM13 (1997) is one of the most underrated WM matches
- His AEW work as a mentor and teacher reflects a career dedicated to the industry beyond his own fame
- His genuine friendship with Cody Rhodes in AEW — as brothers — gave the Finish the Story arc its family dimension
