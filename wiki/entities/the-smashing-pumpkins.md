# The Smashing Pumpkins

American alternative rock band formed in Chicago in 1988. Led by Billy Corgan, the band became one of the most commercially successful and critically acclaimed bands of the 1990s.

## Albums

- [Siamese Dream (1993)](../music/albums/siamese-dream.md) — Second studio album; 4× platinum. Widely regarded as one of the greatest albums of the 1990s. Ranked #20 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
