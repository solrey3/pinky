# Sun Tzu

Legendary Chinese military strategist and author of *The Art of War*, traditionally dated to the 5th century BCE. His identity is debated — some scholars consider the text a compilation by multiple authors. Regardless, the work is the foundational text of military strategy and has been applied to fields as diverse as business, sports, negotiation, and politics.
