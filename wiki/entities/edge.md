# Edge

#26.

**Real Name:** Adam Copeland
**Born:** 1973, Toronto ON
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Active:** 1992–2012, 2020–2023
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #26 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#26. The most psychologically complex character in WWE history — from Brood member to tag specialist to Rated-R Superstar to Ultimate Opportunist to Heel-of-the-Decade. Eleven-time WWE Champion. His feuds with John Cena, Randy Orton, and The Undertaker are among the best long-form storytelling in WWE history. His retirement (2012) due to neck injury and comeback (2020) is wrestling's greatest medical story.

## Highlights

- 11x WWE Champion; founding member of TLC matches
- His TLC matches (with Hardy Boyz and Dudley Boyz) built a stipulation sub-genre
- His feud with John Cena (2006–2007) is the best heel run of the 2000s
- The Edge/Undertaker Hell in a Cell matches are among the best in the stipulation's history
- His 2020 return from bone fusion neck surgery to main event WrestleMania is medically remarkable
