# Miles Davis

American jazz trumpeter, bandleader, and composer from Alton, Illinois. One of the most influential musicians of the 20th century, Davis was at the forefront of several major jazz movements including bebop, cool jazz, hard bop, modal jazz, and jazz fusion.

- [Kind of Blue (1959)](../music/albums/kind-of-blue.md) — The definitive modal jazz masterpiece; greatest jazz album ever recorded.
