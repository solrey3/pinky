# Jimmy Cliff

Jamaican ska and reggae musician, multi-instrumentalist, singer, and actor. Best known for his roles in the film *The Harder They Come* and his contributions to its soundtrack.

## Albums

- [The Harder They Come (soundtrack) (1972)](../music/albums/the-harder-they-come.md) — Soundtrack album that introduced reggae to international audiences. Ranked #5 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
