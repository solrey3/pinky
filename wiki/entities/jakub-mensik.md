# Jakub Menšík

**Nationality:** Czech  
**Born:** 2005, Přerov, Czech Republic  
**ATP Ranking:** ~26th seed (Roland Garros 2026)  
**Active:** ~2022–present

## Summary

Czech ATP rising talent, part of the same next-generation wave that includes Joao Fonseca, Carlos Alcaraz, and Holger Rune. At the **2026 Roland Garros**, Menšík (seeded 26th) defeated **Joao Fonseca** in the quarterfinals — eliminating the tournament's breakout star whose remarkable run included a five-set upset of Novak Djokovic (3-seed) in the third round. The Menšík-Fonseca result is not an upset of similar magnitude; both players represent the same next-gen cohort. What it illustrates is the *depth* of the wave: the player who ended Djokovic's French Open run was himself eliminated by another peer-level 20-something.

Menšík has the physical profile for hard surfaces and clay alike — solid serve, good footwork, and an aggressive baseline game. His reaching the Roland Garros 2026 semifinals from the 26th seed is the kind of result that forces a conversation about where he fits in the ATP hierarchy going forward.
