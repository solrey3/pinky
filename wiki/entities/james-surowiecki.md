# James Surowiecki

American journalist and author best known for *The Wisdom of Crowds* (2004). He was a staff writer at *The New Yorker* for many years, covering business, finance, and economics. His work explores how groups make decisions, why markets work (and fail), and how dispersed knowledge can be aggregated into collective intelligence.
