# Nirvana

American rock band formed in Aberdeen, Washington, in 1987. Founded by lead singer and guitarist Kurt Cobain and bassist Krist Novoselic, the band went through a succession of drummers before recruiting Dave Grohl in 1990.

## Albums

- [Nevermind (1991)](../music/albums/nevermind.md) — Second studio album; propelled grunge into the mainstream. 13× platinum. National Recording Registry. Ranked #12 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
