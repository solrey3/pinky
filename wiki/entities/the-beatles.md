# The Beatles

English rock band formed in Liverpool in 1960. With members John Lennon, Paul McCartney, George Harrison and Ringo Starr, they became the most influential band in the history of popular music.

## Albums

- [Revolver (1966)](../music/albums/revolver.md) — Seventh studio album; marked their most overt use of studio technology and the start of their psychedelic period. Ranked #10 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
