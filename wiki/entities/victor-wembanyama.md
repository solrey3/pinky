# Victor Wembanyama

French NBA center for the San Antonio Spurs, widely regarded as a generational talent — an unprecedented combination of size (7'4"), wingspan, shooting range, shot-blocking, and ball-handling. The 2023 #1 overall draft pick. In the 2026 NBA Playoffs, he announced himself on the national stage by forcing double overtime against the Oklahoma City Thunder in Game 1, including a half-court buzzer beater. As of May 26, 2026, the Spurs are still competing in the WCF against the OKC Thunder; Spurs fans have been bringing an Oscar to games to mock SGA's foul-drawing theatrics.
