# Victor Wembanyama

French NBA center for the San Antonio Spurs, widely regarded as a generational talent — an unprecedented combination of size (7'4"), wingspan, shooting range, shot-blocking, and ball-handling. The 2023 #1 overall draft pick. In the 2026 NBA Playoffs, he announced himself on the national stage by forcing double overtime against the Oklahoma City Thunder in Game 1, including a half-court buzzer beater. As of the May 30 morning dispatch, the stage is set for a winner-take-all **Game 7 at Oklahoma City tonight (8 PM EDT, NBC)** — but with a dramatically altered landscape: **Jalen Williams (OKC's second-best player) is OUT**, and **Ajay Mitchell is also OUT**. The Thunder, hosting Game 7 with home-court advantage, go in short-handed. Wembanyama and the Spurs, who have physically imposed their will whenever SGA is held in check, enter this game as legitimate favorites.
