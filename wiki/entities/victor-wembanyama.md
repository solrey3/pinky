# Victor Wembanyama

French NBA center for the San Antonio Spurs, widely regarded as a generational talent — an unprecedented combination of size (7'4"), wingspan, shooting range, shot-blocking, and ball-handling. The 2023 #1 overall draft pick. In the 2026 NBA Playoffs, he announced himself on the national stage by forcing double overtime against the Oklahoma City Thunder in Game 1, including a half-court buzzer beater. As of May 29, 2026, the Western Conference Finals is **tied 3-3** — the Spurs demolished OKC 118–91 in Game 6 at home to force a winner-take-all Game 7. SGA was locked up all night. House of Highlights captured Wembanyama getting physical with Lu Dort and a moment described as "ordering a hit on McCain." The series that many thought was over after Game 5 is now entirely alive.
