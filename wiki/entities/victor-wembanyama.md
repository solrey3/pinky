# Victor Wembanyama

French NBA center for the San Antonio Spurs, widely regarded as a generational talent — an unprecedented combination of size (7'4"), wingspan, shooting range, shot-blocking, and ball-handling. The 2023 #1 overall draft pick. In the 2026 NBA Playoffs, he announced himself on the national stage by forcing double overtime against the Oklahoma City Thunder in Game 1, including a half-court buzzer beater. As of May 27, 2026, the Spurs trail the OKC Thunder 2-3 in the WCF after a 114–127 Game 5 loss. Game 6 heads back to San Antonio — Wembanyama and company still alive but on the brink. House of Highlights captured a moment where Wembanyama bumped Chet Holmgren walking to the bench after Game 5, Jaylin Williams visibly laughing.
