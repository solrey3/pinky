# Nate Silver

American statistician, poker player, and founder of FiveThirtyEight. Silver gained fame for his accurate political forecasting models and has applied statistical thinking to baseball, poker, weather, earthquakes, and terrorism. His work champions Bayesian reasoning, probabilistic thinking, and the humility of expressing uncertainty rather than false certainty.
