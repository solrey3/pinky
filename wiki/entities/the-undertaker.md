# The Undertaker

#6 all-time.

**Real Name:** Mark Calaway
**Born:** 1965, Houston TX
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1984–2020
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #6 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#6 all-time. The most consistent long-term character in professional wrestling history. The Deadman gimmick debuted in 1990 and was still drawing main event money in 2020 — a 30-year arc of a single character without equivalent in any entertainment medium. His WrestleMania undefeated streak (21-0) is wrestling's most sacred record. Paul Bearer as his manager is the perfect supporting character.

## Highlights

- 21-0 WrestleMania record (ended by Brock Lesnar at WM30 — a genuine shock)
- The Hell in a Cell matches — especially with Mick Foley (1998) and Shawn Michaels (1997) — defined the stipulation
- Reinvented from supernatural Deadman to biker 'American Bad Ass' and back; both worked
- The Buried Alive and Casket matches built an entire sub-genre of stipulation wrestling
- His retirement ceremony ('Deadman's Last Ride') at WM36 was genuinely moving
