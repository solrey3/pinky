# FFmpeg

Open-source multimedia framework and command-line toolchain for recording, converting, streaming, inspecting, and processing audio/video. In this wiki it matters both as a daily media/DJ-engine utility and as a large, security-sensitive parsing surface: it touches untrusted media formats, codecs, containers, and metadata.
