# Pulp

English rock band formed in Sheffield in 1978. A key figure in the Britpop movement of the 1990s, led by frontman Jarvis Cocker.

## Albums

- [Different Class (1995)](../music/albums/different-class.md) — Fifth studio album; Britpop masterpiece that entered the UK chart at #1 and won the Mercury Prize. Ranked #4 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
