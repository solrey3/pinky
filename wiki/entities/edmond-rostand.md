# Edmond Rostand

French poet and dramatist associated with neo-romanticism. His most famous play, *Cyrano de Bergerac* (1897), owes a clear debt to the character archetype of the noble, idealistic fool established by Cervantes in *Don Quixote*.

**Lived:** 1868–1918  
**Notable Work:** *Cyrano de Bergerac*
