# Carmelo Anthony

The greatest pure scorer in the generation between Jordan/Kobe and LeBron/Durant.

**Born:** 1984, Brooklyn NY
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF/PF
**Active:** 2003–2022
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The greatest pure scorer in the generation between Jordan/Kobe and LeBron/Durant. One scoring title, ten All-Star selections, three Olympic gold medals. Melo's mid-range game is the most complete in basketball after Jordan and Kobe. Never won a championship — the choices he made (individual scoring vs. system fit) explain why.

## Highlights

- 1x scoring champion (2013); 10x All-Star; 3x Olympic gold medalist
- His 62-point game against the Bobcats (2014) is the single-game scoring record in Madison Square Garden history
- The most technically complete mid-range scorer of his generation
- His decision to leave Denver (2011) and his Miami stint (2018) illustrate the gap between his individual talent and championship construction
- His Olympics legacy (three gold medals) is the most underrated part of his basketball biography
