# Drew McIntyre

#65.

**Real Name:** Drew Galloway
**Born:** 1985, Ayr, Scotland
**Nationality:** Scottish
**Active:** 2001–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #65 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#65. The first Scottish-born WWE World Champion. McIntyre's career is a genuine redemption arc — released by WWE in 2014 and told by Triple H he would never make it back, he rebuilt his reputation in the independents (Evolve, ICW, TNA) and returned to win the NXT Championship and then the WWE Championship at WM36. His Royal Rumble 2020 win set up one of the better organic championship stories in WWE.

## Highlights

- WWE Champion; NXT Champion
- His WM36 WWE Championship win (empty arena, COVID) is one of wrestling's most unusual major title changes
- His Royal Rumble 2020 win from #16 entry is the best Rumble performance of the 2020s
- His ICW and Evolve work after WWE release showed that he could work without the company's infrastructure
- The Claymore Kick is wrestling's most convincing single-kick finish — the setup and delivery are near-perfect
- His feud with CM Punk (2023–2024) produced some of the most buzzed-about WWE matches in years
