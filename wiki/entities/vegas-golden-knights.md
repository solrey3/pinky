# Vegas Golden Knights

NHL franchise based in Las Vegas, NV. The league's most successful expansion team in the modern era — entered the league in 2017 and reached the Stanley Cup Finals in their inaugural season. Won the Stanley Cup in 2023. Built on a defensive identity, opportunistic scoring, and a franchise culture that embraced Las Vegas's entertainment DNA from day one. In 2025-26, they swept the Colorado Avalanche in the Western Conference Finals and entered the Stanley Cup Finals as the Western representative.
