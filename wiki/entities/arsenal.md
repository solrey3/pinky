# Arsenal FC

London-based Premier League club, founded 1886. One of England's most storied football institutions. Known for the Invincibles season (2003-04, 49-match unbeaten run under Arsène Wenger), the Emirates Stadium era, and a long wait to return to the summit of European football. In the 2025-26 UEFA Champions League campaign, Arsenal went **8-0-0** through the group stage and knockout rounds — the most dominant UCL run by an English club in the modern era — and **reached the UCL Final, where they drew 1-1 with Paris Saint-Germain and lost 4-3 on penalties**. The campaign ends in heartbreak at the final hurdle.
