# Bret Hart (The Hitman)

#4 all-time.

**Real Name:** Bret Hart
**Born:** 1957, Calgary AB
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Active:** 1978–2000
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #4 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#4 all-time. The most technically sound wrestler in North American history. A product of Stu Hart's Dungeon in Calgary, Hart's submission-based style and scientific wrestling philosophy was the standard against which all technical work was measured in the 1990s. His double-turn with Steve Austin at WrestleMania 13 is the greatest booking decision in wrestling history.

## Highlights

- 5x WWF Champion; 2x WCW Champion
- WrestleMania 13 with Steve Austin — a match that simultaneously turned Austin face and Hart heel using pure storytelling
- The Sharpshooter is one of wrestling's most iconic submission holds
- His Excellence of Execution character (the good guy who had a point about being underappreciated) anticipated the anti-hero era
- The Montreal Screwjob (1997) altered WWE's entire corporate mythology
