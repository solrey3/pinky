# Joel and Ethan Coen

The greatest writing-directing team in American cinema history.

**Born:** 1954, 1957 — St. Louis Park MN
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1984–present
**Profession:** Film directors, writers, producers

## Summary

The greatest writing-directing team in American cinema history. Blood Simple, Raising Arizona, Miller's Crossing, Barton Fink, The Hudsucker Proxy, Fargo, The Big Lebowski, No Country for Old Men, True Grit, Inside Llewyn Davis — their filmography is the most consistent in American cinema. Fargo won Academy Awards for Best Director and Screenplay.

## Highlights

- Fargo (1996) — in personal film rankings; Academy Awards for Best Director and Original Screenplay
- No Country for Old Men (2007) — Academy Award for Best Picture, Director, Screenplay, Supporting Actor; their most critically acclaimed film
- The Big Lebowski (1998) — the most durable cult film in American cinema; every line quotable
- Blood Simple (1984) — debut film that established their visual style fully-formed
- Their screenplay quality across 20+ films is more consistent than any other writing team in cinema history
