# Dwyane Wade

The greatest SG of his generation outside of Kobe.

**Born:** 1982, Chicago IL
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 2003–2019
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 5 (#21)

## Summary

The greatest SG of his generation outside of Kobe. Three championships (2006, 2012, 2013 with the Heat), one Finals MVP. Wade's 2006 Finals performance (34.7 ppg, getting to the free throw line constantly) is the most clutch individual championship performance of his era. His partnership with LeBron and Bosh (2010–2014) built one of the NBA's most star-powered teams.

## Highlights

- 3x NBA Champion (2006, 2012, 2013); 1x Finals MVP (2006)
- His 2006 Finals performance (34.7 ppg) against Dallas is the most clutch SG championship performance since Jordan
- 13x All-Star; 1x scoring champion (2009)
- His collaboration with LeBron James (2010–2014) is the most successful recruited superteam in NBA history
- His alley-oop, drive-and-finish repertoire at 6'4" set the template for athletic guards in the modern era
