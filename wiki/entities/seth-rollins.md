# Seth Rollins

#16.

**Real Name:** Colby Lopez
**Born:** 1986, Davenport IA
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2005–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #16 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#16. The most complete working wrestler on the WWE roster. Rollins can work any style — technical, brawling, high-flying — and has produced consistently great matches across every opponent. His Shield run (with Reigns and Ambrose) is the best faction debut in modern WWE. The Curb Stomp is wrestling's most visually impressive finisher. His ring gear is legitimately avant-garde.

## Highlights

- Multiple WWE/Universal World Champion; Money in the Bank winner
- The Shield's debut (2012) and dissolution (2014) is the best faction arc in modern WWE
- His 2015 run as Authority champion and US Champion was the most creatively productive in WWE that year
- His WrestleMania 31 cash-in during Lesnar vs. Reigns is the greatest MITB cash-in in history
- The Visionary's ability to have different types of great matches makes him uniquely versatile
