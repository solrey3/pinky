# Miguel de Cervantes

Spanish writer widely regarded as the greatest writer in the Spanish language and one of the world's pre-eminent novelists. His magnum opus, *Don Quixote*, is often cited as the first modern novel and one of the most influential works of literature ever written.

**Lived:** 1547–1616  
**Notable Work:** *Don Quixote* (1605, 1615)
