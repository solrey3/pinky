# Tiago Forte

Author, educator, and productivity consultant. Creator of the PARA method (Projects, Areas, Resources, Archives) and the *Building a Second Brain* methodology. His work focuses on applying digital tools and organizational frameworks to personal knowledge management, creativity, and productivity.
