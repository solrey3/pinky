# Mark Twain

American writer, humorist, and satirist. Widely considered one of the greatest American writers, he famously credited *Don Quixote* with having "swept the world's admiration for the mediaeval chivalry-silliness out of existence."

**Lived:** 1835–1910  
**Notable Works:** *The Adventures of Tom Sawyer*, *Adventures of Huckleberry Finn*
