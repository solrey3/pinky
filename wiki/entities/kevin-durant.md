# Kevin Durant

The most skilled offensive player in NBA history.

**Born:** 1988, Washington DC
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF/PF
**Active:** 2007–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 3 (#13)

## Summary

The most skilled offensive player in NBA history. Durant's combination of size (6'10"), shooting range, shot creation, and touch from every area of the court is unprecedented. Two championships (with Golden State, 2017 and 2018), two Finals MVPs, one regular-season MVP. His 35.4 scoring average in the 2022 NBA Playoffs is the highest in history.

## Highlights

- 2x NBA Champion (2017, 2018); 2x Finals MVP; 1x regular-season MVP
- 4x NBA scoring champion; 13x All-Star
- His ability to score from any spot on the floor at 6'10" is genuinely unprecedented — no defender has a reliable counter
- His 35.4 scoring average in the 2022 NBA Playoffs is the highest playoff scoring average in NBA history
- His Brooklyn Nets era (despite the team's dysfunction) produced some of the most individually excellent basketball of his career
