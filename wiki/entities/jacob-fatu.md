# Jacob Fatu

**Real Name:** Jacob Fatu  
**Nationality:** Samoan-American  
**Active:** 2014–present (NXT / main roster)  
**Affiliation:** The Bloodline (WWE)  
**Profession:** Professional wrestler (WWE)

## Summary

Member of the Anoa'i wrestling family — one of the most storied lineages in the business (The Rock, Roman Reigns, Umaga, Rikishi, the Usos). Jacob Fatu is a legitimately explosive big man: 285 lbs who can do moonsaults and cartwheels, with a intensity and physicality that made him the centerpiece of the next-generation Bloodline faction in WWE. His character was cast as the enforcer / heir apparent challenging Roman Reigns for control of the Bloodline during Reigns's extended absence. On **May 31, 2026**, at WWE Clash in Italy in Turin, he lost to Roman Reigns in **Tribal Combat** — and was forced to acknowledge Reigns, ending the Bloodline civil war.
