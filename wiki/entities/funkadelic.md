# Funkadelic

American funk rock band formed in Plainfield, New Jersey in 1968. Led by George Clinton, the band was the funk-rock counterpart to Parliament, pioneering the P-Funk sound.

## Albums

- [One Nation Under a Groove (1978)](../music/albums/one-nation-under-a-groove.md) — Tenth studio album; their most commercially successful. Platinum. Ranked #13 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
