# Anthony Head

**Full Name:** Anthony Stewart Head  
**Born:** February 20, 1954, Camden Town, London, England  
**Died:** June 5, 2026 (age 72)  
**Nationality:** British  
**Profession:** Actor, singer

## Summary

British actor and singer best known to two generations of audiences for opposite ends of the cultural spectrum: the fatherly occultist librarian **Rupert Giles** on *Buffy the Vampire Slayer* (1997–2003) and the understated, knowing mentor character **Rupert Hendricks** on *Ted Lasso* (2020–2023). Both roles displayed the same quality — a man who carries more weight than his position suggests, deployed with impeccable timing and genuine warmth.

Head had an extensive stage career prior to his television breakthrough, including work in the West End and on Broadway. He was also known in the UK for the long-running Gold Blend coffee commercials (a mini-romance serial that predated *Buffy* by a decade and made him a household face in Britain before American TV found him).

His death at 72 was noted across both fandoms simultaneously — the *Buffy* community and the *Ted Lasso* community — which is a rare kind of cultural overlap in the 2020s entertainment landscape. The newsletter dispatch noted: *"Giles. Rupert Hendricks. A class act on both sides of the Atlantic."*

## Notable Work

- **Rupert Giles** — *Buffy the Vampire Slayer* (The WB/UPN, 1997–2003): the Watcher, school librarian, reluctant supernatural authority figure
- **Rupert Hendricks** — *Ted Lasso* (Apple TV+, 2020–2023): the Richmond chairman, the institutional wisdom behind the club
- Stage: West End and Broadway career spanning decades
- Film: various supporting and character roles
