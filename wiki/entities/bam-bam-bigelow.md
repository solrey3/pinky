# Bam Bam Bigelow

#48.

**Real Name:** Scott Bigelow
**Born:** 1961, Asbury Park NJ
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1985–2007
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #48 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#48. The greatest big man with athletic gifts in wrestling history. A 400-pound man who did moonsaults from the top rope, backflips, and could outrun smaller wrestlers. His match with Lawrence Taylor at WM11 is the best celebrity match in wrestling history. His Asbury Park, New Jersey connection is a specific biographical note.

## Highlights

- ECW TV Champion; multiple NWA/WWF mid-card champion
- His WM11 match with Lawrence Taylor is the best celebrity main event in WM history
- His run in ECW (1997–1998) is the best work of his career — he had genuine respect in that environment
- The Beast from the East was genuinely mobile for a man of his size — moonsaults from the top rope
- His New Jersey hometown connection is a personal one for this list
