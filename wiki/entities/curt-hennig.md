# Curt Hennig (Mr. Perfect)

#58.

**Real Name:** Curt Hennig
**Born:** 1958, Robbinsdale MN
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1980–2003
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #58 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#58. The greatest IC Title carrier in WWE history. Mr. Perfect's character — a man who claimed (and often demonstrated) literal perfection in everything he did — was the most self-contained gimmick in wrestling. His vignettes (perfect golf, perfect football catches, perfect bowling) are the best character-building segments in wrestling history. His match with Bret Hart at SummerSlam 1991 is the best IC title match ever.

## Highlights

- Multiple AWA/WWF Intercontinental Champion
- His SummerSlam 1991 match with Bret Hart is the best IC Championship match in WWE history
- The Mr. Perfect vignettes (1989) set the standard for character-introduction television in wrestling
- His tag team with Larry Hennig and his AWA championship run established family wrestling dynasty credibility
- He died in 2003 from acute cocaine toxicity — his death preceded a series of wrestling losses that revealed systemic issues
