# Ricky Steamboat

#12 all-time.

**Real Name:** Richard Blood
**Born:** 1953, Honolulu HI
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1976–1994
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #12 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#12 all-time. The greatest babyface in-ring performer of his era. The WrestleMania III match with Randy Savage is universally cited as the bout that proved WWE could produce world-class wrestling. His 1989 trilogy with Ric Flair set the technical standard for wrestling storytelling. The Dragon character was built around genuine martial arts skills and graceful athleticism.

## Highlights

- NWA/WCW World Heavyweight Champion
- WM3 vs. Savage is on every 'greatest matches ever' list — a 14-minute masterwork
- The 1989 Flair trilogy set the template for 60-minute epic storytelling in wrestling
- His technical style influenced every 'pure wrestling' movement that followed
- Never had a truly bad match in a 15-year career
