# Dominique Wilkins

The Human Highlight Film.

**Born:** 1960, Paris, France
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF
**Active:** 1982–1999
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The Human Highlight Film. The most explosive dunker of his era and the player who made the Slam Dunk Contest the NBA's most-watched non-game event. Multiple scoring titles, nine All-Star selections. His dunk contest battle with Michael Jordan (1988, both scoring perfect 50s) is the greatest single event in All-Star Weekend history.

## Highlights

- 1x scoring champion (1986); 9x All-Star
- His 1988 Slam Dunk Contest battle with Michael Jordan — both scoring 50s, Jordan winning controversially — is the greatest dunk contest in history
- The Human Highlight Film nickname is the most accurate in basketball — every game had two or three plays that didn't belong in the human achievement range
- His 47-point game against Detroit (1988 ECF Game 7) in a losing effort is the best individual performance in a lost playoff game
- His late-career (33) Achilles tendon tear recovery is the greatest comeback story in basketball before Kawhi's
