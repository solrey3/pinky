# Kurt Angle (The Olympic Hero / It's True, It's True)

#25.

**Real Name:** Kurt Angle
**Born:** 1968, Pittsburgh PA
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1996–2019
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / Olympic wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #25 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#25. The only person who won an Olympic gold medal (wrestling, Atlanta 1996) and then became one of the top five in-ring performers in WWE history. His transition from amateur wrestling to professional wrestling is the most successful in history. His matches with Benoit, Lesnar, Shawn Michaels, and Eddie Guerrero span every style and are consistently great.

## Highlights

- Olympic gold medalist (1996); multiple WWE World Champion
- His ankle lock is the most credible submission hold in wrestling — it comes from legitimate technique
- The Angle/Lesnar WM19 match is one of the greatest pure wrestling matches in WM history
- His comedic segments (the milk truck, the 'YOU SUCK' chant to his own entrance music) showed range beyond pure wrestling
- His Vengeance 2005 match with Shawn Michaels is ★★★★¾ by most estimates
