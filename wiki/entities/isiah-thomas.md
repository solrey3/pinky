# Isiah Thomas

The greatest bad boy and the fiercest competitor of the 1980s NBA.

**Born:** 1961, Chicago IL
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 1981–1994
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 5 (#22)

## Summary

The greatest bad boy and the fiercest competitor of the 1980s NBA. Thomas led the Detroit Pistons to back-to-back championships (1989, 1990) against a series of opponents — Jordan, Bird, Magic — who he had to beat at their peaks. His 1988 Finals performance (43 points in Game 6 on an injured ankle) is the most gutsy individual performance in Finals history.

## Highlights

- 2x NBA Champion (1989, 1990 Detroit Pistons); 2x Finals MVP
- His 1988 Finals Game 6 (43 points with a sprained ankle) is one of the most gutsy individual performances in NBA history
- 12x All-Star; 1x All-Star Game MVP (1984); 5x All-NBA
- His Bad Boy Pistons are the most physically intimidating dynasty in NBA history
- His feud with Michael Jordan is basketball's defining small-man rivalry — he prevented Jordan from winning for three consecutive years
