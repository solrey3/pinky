# Pau Gasol

The most technically skilled European big man in NBA history.

**Born:** 1980, Barcelona, Spain
**Nationality:** Spanish
**Position:** PF/C
**Active:** 2001–2019
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The most technically skilled European big man in NBA history. Two championships (2009, 2010 Lakers), six All-Star selections. His partnership with Kobe Bryant revived a Lakers franchise and produced the most tactically sophisticated offense in the Kobe era. His footwork and passing from the high post are the model for modern stretch-4 play.

## Highlights

- 2x NBA Champion (2009, 2010 Los Angeles Lakers); 6x All-Star
- His partnership with Kobe in back-to-back championships is the best star-sidekick relationship of the 2000s
- The most complete skill set (passing, shooting, defense, footwork) of any European big man in NBA history
- His Olympic career (2x silver, 1x gold with Spain) is the most decorated international basketball career of his era
- His Hall of Fame career is the foundation of Spanish basketball's international credibility
