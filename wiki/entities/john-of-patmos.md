# John of Patmos

Traditional author of the Book of Revelation, the final book of the New Testament. Identified by early church tradition as John the Apostle, though modern scholars debate this attribution. He wrote while exiled on the island of Patmos (modern Greece) around 95 CE, producing a visionary, symbolic text that has shaped Christian eschatology, art, and political theology for two millennia.
