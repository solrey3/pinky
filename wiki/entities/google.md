# Google

American technology company and AI lab/operator behind Search, Android, Google Cloud, and the Gemini model family. In the June 2026 newsletter stream, Google appears both as a direct AI builder and as model infrastructure for other major platforms. Gemini is showing up in two lanes at once: as hidden plumbing for platform partners and as direct user-facing utility such as voice translation.
