# Daniel Myers

Basketball statistician who created [[Box Plus/Minus]] (BPM), a foundational plus-minus metric that inspired the box component of [[RAPTOR]]. BPM was designed by fitting regression coefficients from traditional box score statistics against long-term RAPM.
