# André the Giant

#68.

**Real Name:** André Roussimoff
**Born:** 1946, Grenoble, France
**Nationality:** French
**Active:** 1966–1992
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #68 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#68. The most physically unique figure in wrestling history. André's gigantism (7'4", 500+ lbs) made him a genuine marvel — a man who traveled the world for 25 years as a literal attraction. His match with Hogan at WM3 (93,000 at the Silverdome) is wrestling's most-attended event. His participation in The Princess Bride (Fezzik) is his cultural legacy beyond wrestling.

## Highlights

- WWF Champion (briefly, 1988); undefeated for 15 years
- His WM3 match with Hogan — the slam on a man this large — is wrestling's defining physical achievement
- His 15-year undefeated streak (1966–1981) is the most remarkable competitive run in wrestling
- Fezzik in The Princess Bride showed genuine warmth and presence beyond wrestling
- His death in 1993 from congestive heart failure was the first death of a wrestling icon
