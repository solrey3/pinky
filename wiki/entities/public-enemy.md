# Public Enemy

American hip hop group formed in 1985 on Long Island, New York. Known for their politically charged lyrics, aggressive production by the Bomb Squad, and dynamic performances by Chuck D and Flavor Flav.

## Albums

- [It Takes a Nation of Millions to Hold Us Back (1988)](../music/albums/it-takes-a-nation-of-millions.md) — Second studio album; dense Bomb Squad production and revolutionary sociopolitical rhetoric. Ranked #1 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
