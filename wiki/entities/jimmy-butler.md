# Jimmy Butler

The most competitive player in the modern NBA outside of LeBron and Jordan.

**Born:** 1989, Houston TX
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF/SG
**Active:** 2011–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The most competitive player in the modern NBA outside of LeBron and Jordan. Six All-Star selections, two Finals appearances (2020 and 2023 Miami Heat). Butler's ability to take over games in the fourth quarter while playing defense at an All-Defensive level is his defining characteristic. His Game 6 performance (40 pts) against the Lakers in the 2020 Finals forced a Game 6.

## Highlights

- 6x All-Star; 1x Defensive Player of the Year finalist
- His 2020 NBA Finals Game 6 performance (40 pts, 11 reb, 11 ast, 3 stl) against the Lakers is the most complete individual Finals performance of the 2020s
- His 2023 Heat run to the Finals (as an 8-seed) is the most improbable Finals appearance in the three-point era
- His conditioning and late-game scoring are the most reliable in the modern NBA for a wing
- His contentious exit from Minnesota, Philadelphia, and Chicago shows the cost of his competitive standards
