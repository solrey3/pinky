# Dennis Rodman

The Worm.

**Born:** 1961, Trenton NJ
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PF
**Active:** 1986–2000
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The Worm. Five NBA championships (2 with Detroit, 3 with Chicago). The greatest rebounder of the modern era — led the league in rebounds seven consecutive seasons. Rodman's defensive IQ (the study of opposing shooters' rebound tendencies) was unique. His personal life (Carmen Electra, North Korea) has defined his public identity as much as his basketball.

## Highlights

- 5x NBA Champion (1989, 1990 Pistons; 1996, 1997, 1998 Bulls); 2x Defensive Player of the Year
- Led the league in rebounding seven consecutive seasons — the most in NBA history
- His study of how specific shooters' missed shots bounced is the most analytical approach to rebounding in history
- His 1996 Finals performance (defending Shaq while also averaging 14.9 rebounds per game) is the greatest defensive rebounding performance in Finals history
- He is the most fascinating human being who has ever played in the NBA
