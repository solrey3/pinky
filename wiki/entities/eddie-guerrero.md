# Eddie Guerrero

#29.

**Real Name:** Eduardo Guerrero
**Born:** 1967, El Paso TX
**Nationality:** Mexican-American
**Active:** 1987–2005
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #29 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#29. 'Lie, Cheat and Steal' — Eddie Guerrero's character was a masterclass in making a morally suspect approach beloved by using humor and humanity to frame it. His technical ability was world-class, his character work transcended the ring. His friendship with Chris Benoit and their WM XX celebration was wrestling's most joyful moment before becoming one of its saddest. He died in 2005 at age 38 from heart failure.

## Highlights

- WWE World Heavyweight Champion (WM XX)
- His matches with Malenko in WCW (1996–1997) are the best technical wrestling in that company's history
- The Eddie/Benoit WM XX double celebration is one of the purest moments of joy in wrestling history
- His tag team chemistry with Tajiri and (later) Rey Mysterio showed his ability to elevate others
- His death in 2005 was the first major WWE-era star death and reshaped how WWE dealt with talent wellness
