# Anthony Davis

The most physically versatile big man in modern NBA history.

**Born:** 1993, Chicago IL
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PF/C
**Active:** 2012–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The most physically versatile big man in modern NBA history. One championship (2020 Lakers), one Finals MVP (lost on a technicality — LeBron won it), eight All-Star selections. AD's ability to guard all five positions while scoring from inside and outside makes him the defensive anchor of any elite team he joins.

## Highlights

- 1x NBA Champion (2020 Los Angeles Lakers); 8x All-Star; 1x DPOY
- His 2020 NBA Bubble season (26.1 ppg, 9.3 rpg, 3.1 bpk) while guarding every position is his most complete season
- His partnership with LeBron James in the Bubble is the most concentrated period of two-player dominance in the modern game
- The most versatile defensive player in the modern era — can legitimately guard any position from 1 through 5
- His injury history (multiple seasons limited below 50 games) has prevented a more definitive championship resume
