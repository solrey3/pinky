# Sol Ruca

**Real Name:** Unreleased (works as Sol Ruca)  
**Nationality:** American  
**Active:** 2022–present (NXT debut 2022)  
**Profession:** Professional wrestler (WWE)

## Summary

WWE NXT and main roster talent known for a background in gymnastics and skateboarding — an unusual combination that translates directly into her ring style. Her signature Sol Snatcher (a forward roll cutter executed with springboard momentum) is one of the most visually distinctive finishers in the current roster. She debuted in NXT in 2022 and was called up to the main roster in 2024. On **May 31, 2026**, at WWE Clash in Italy in Turin, she defeated Becky Lynch to win the **WWE Women's Intercontinental Championship** — her first title on the main roster and one of the more significant star-making moments of the 2026 WWE calendar.

## Key Moment

- **2026-05-31 — WWE Clash in Italy (Turin):** Defeats Becky Lynch for the WWE Women's Intercontinental Championship. The clean win over one of the most established women's wrestlers in WWE history validates her as a legitimate championship-level talent. Charlotte Flair attacking Jade Cargill backstage on the same card shifts the division's attention, but Ruca's title win is the most consequential long-term result for the women's division from this show.
