# Ben Mezrich

American author known for narrative nonfiction about high-stakes subcultures. *Bringing Down the House* (2002) was adapted into the film *21*. Mezrich specializes in finding stories where brilliant young people use mathematics or technology to exploit institutional blind spots — whether casinos, markets, or social systems.
