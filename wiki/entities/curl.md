Command-line data transfer tool and underlying library ecosystem used across scripts, package managers, CI jobs, containers, and operating-system plumbing. In this wiki, curl already exists as a cheatsheet source; the June 2026 newsletter stream makes it an entity because vulnerability-intake policy for a ubiquitous dependency is itself operational security infrastructure.
