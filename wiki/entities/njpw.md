# NJPW (New Japan Pro-Wrestling)

Founded in 1972 by Antonio Inoki, New Japan Pro-Wrestling is the premier puroresu promotion and one of the most significant wrestling organizations in the world. Known for strong-style wrestling, the G1 Climax tournament, and the Intercontinental and IWGP World Heavyweight Championships. NJPW's 2010s run under Bushiroad ownership — built on Kazuchika Okada's IWGP reigns, Kenny Omega's matches, and the British J Cup — produced what many fans and journalists consider the best wrestling in the world from roughly 2015–2019.

**Ownership History:**
- 1972–1989: Antonio Inoki era
- 2012–2026: Bushiroad era — significant globalization, talent exports to AEW, STRONG brand launch
- 2026–present: TV Asahi + CyberAgent era — Bushiroad transferred all shares in May 2026
