# Rob Van Dam

#28.

**Real Name:** Robert Szatkowski
**Born:** 1970, Battle Creek MI
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1990–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #28 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#28. The most athletically gifted performer in ECW history and the man most responsible for that company's mainstream crossover. His five-star frog splash, rolling thunder, and Van Terminator are the most genuinely acrobatic moves in American wrestling. His ECW and WWE title runs in 2006 validated the ECW brand's credibility for a generation of fans.

## Highlights

- ECW Champion; WWE Champion (Money in the Bank cash-in, 2006)
- His TV matches in ECW (1996–2001) built his reputation as the most exciting weekly performer in wrestling
- His MITB cash-in on Cena in front of a sold-out ECW-crowd WWE Raw is one of the great crowd moments in wrestling
- The Van Terminator (corner dropkick from the opposite corner) is a stunt that no one else has replicated
- His matches with Jerry Lynn (ECW, 1999) are the best tag-style singles matches of the era
