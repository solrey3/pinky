# The King of Harts / Black Hart

#42.

**Real Name:** Owen Hart
**Born:** 1965, Calgary AB
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Active:** 1986–1999
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #42 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#42. Potentially the most tragic story in wrestling — the son of Stu Hart and brother of Bret Hart was one of the most technically gifted and genuinely funny performers in wrestling when he died in a ring accident on May 23, 1999. He was 34. His King of Harts heel turn against Bret in 1994 is the best sibling storyline in wrestling history.

## Highlights

- WWF European Champion; King of the Ring 1994
- His King of the Ring 1994 win over Bret Hart is the best sibling match in wrestling history
- He was genuinely funny — his comic timing in promos was legitimately better than most trained comedians
- His matches with Bret, Shawn Michaels, and Steve Austin (Survivor Series 1997) were consistently excellent
- His death at Over the Edge 1999 in a harness malfunction is wrestling's most traumatic moment
