# Michael Acevedo

Poker player, coach, and author specializing in game-theory-optimal (GTO) strategy. *Modern Poker Theory* (2019) applies solver outputs to practical poker decision-making, bridging the gap between academic game theory and real-world play. His work operationalizes concepts that were previously theoretical, showing how AI-generated equilibrium strategies can be learned and applied by human players.
