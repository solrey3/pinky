# Christian Cage

#51.

**Real Name:** William Reso
**Born:** 1973, Kitchener ON
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Active:** 1993–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #51 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#51. The most underrated performer in WWE history. Christian's run from mid-card tag team specialist to legitimate main event star across 20+ years is built on consistent excellence rather than a defining moment. His feud with Edge is one of the best friend-turned-rival storylines in wrestling. His AEW run as the elder statesman villain is the best late-career work in wrestling.

## Highlights

- ECW Champion; TNA World Champion; AEW TNT Champion
- His Edge feud (2005 in WWE) produced some of the best pure storytelling of that era
- His AEW Patriarch character (using Luchasaurus/Killswitch) is the most complete heel performance of his career
- His 'one more match' narrative — retiring and coming back for 15 years — is wrestling's longest-running retirement joke
- The Killswitch/Unprettier is one of wrestling's most elegant finishing sequences
