# Jeff Hardy

#77.

**Real Name:** Jeff Hardy
**Born:** 1977, Cameron NC
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1993–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #77 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#77. The most death-defying performer in the history of wrestling's television era. The Swanton Bomb, the Twist of Fate, the ladder dives — Hardy's high-risk work in the TLC matches created the stipulation's mythology alongside the Hardy Boyz/Dudley Boyz/Edge & Christian. His personal struggles (addiction, legal issues) are as documented as his ring work.

## Highlights

- Multiple WWE/TNA Heavyweight Champion; Multiple Tag Champion
- His TLC performances (WM16, SummerSlam 2000) built the ladder match as wrestling's most anticipated stipulation
- The Hardy Boyz tag team is the best high-flying team in WWE history
- His TNA/Impact run (2010–2011) as heel was his most complete character work
- His brother Matt Hardy is wrestling's most enduring sibling team
