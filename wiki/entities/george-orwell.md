# George Orwell

English novelist and essayist (1903–1950), born Eric Arthur Blair. Best known for *Animal Farm* and *1984*, Orwell was a committed democratic socialist who wrote devastating critiques of totalitarianism from both the left and the right. His essays on language and politics remain foundational in discussions of propaganda, surveillance, and the corruption of truth.
