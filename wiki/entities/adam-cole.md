# Adam Cole (Bay Bay)

#39.

**Real Name:** Adam Cole
**Born:** 1989, York PA
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2008–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #39 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#39. The most complete character in NXT history. His NXT Championship reign (2019–2020, 403 days) is the longest in NXT history. His entrance ('ADAM COLE BAY BAY!'), his superkick into Panama Sunrise finish, and his ROH and PWG credibility gave him a complete performer's profile. His AEW run has been interrupted by injury but his matches with Kyle O'Reilly are career highlights.

## Highlights

- NXT Champion (longest reign, 403 days); ROH World Champion
- His NXT TakeOver matches — particularly with Gargano — produced three consecutive POY candidates
- The Panama Sunrise (Canadian Destroyer into a turnbuckle) is the most aesthetically impressive move combination in wrestling
- His ROH world title reign built his reputation as a complete performer outside WWE's developmental
- His match with Kyle O'Reilly at NXT TakeOver Stand & Deliver (2021) is the best pure wrestling match of that year
