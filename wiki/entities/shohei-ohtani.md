# Shohei Ohtani

**Born:** 1994, Iwate, Japan  
**Nationality:** Japanese  
**Team:** Los Angeles Dodgers  
**Position:** Pitcher / Designated Hitter (two-way player)

The most genuinely unprecedented baseball player of the modern era — possibly ever. Shohei Ohtani does what was considered structurally impossible in the professional game: he is simultaneously a front-line starting pitcher and an elite power hitter. His 2023 MVP season (44 HR, 167 strikeouts as a starter) is the statistical argument closed. His $700M/10-year contract with the Dodgers (Dec 2023) is the largest in North American professional sports history.

The two-way player model was theoretical until Ohtani made it empirical. Baseball analytics had largely concluded that the toll of pitching made elite hitting incompatible at the same career stage — Ohtani has refuted that conclusion by compounding output in both directions simultaneously.
