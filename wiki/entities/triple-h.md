# Triple H

#71.

**Real Name:** Paul Levesque
**Born:** 1969, Nashua NH
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1992–2019 (wrestling); ongoing (EVP)
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / WWE executive
**Personal Rank:** #71 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#71. The most powerful figure in the backstage of wrestling for 20 years. His in-ring career (14x World Champion) is complicated by his use of backstage influence — he married Stephanie McMahon, became Chief Operating Officer, and eventually built NXT. His Cerebral Assassin heel character (2000–2004) is legitimately great. His DX run with HBK defined Attitude Era comedy. His booking of NXT is his most lasting achievement.

## Highlights

- 14x World Champion; co-founder DX; NXT creator (Executive)
- The Evolution faction (with Flair, Orton, Batista) is the best multi-generational wrestling faction
- His matches with HBK (three Hell in a Cell and Street Fight matches) are among WWE's best sustained rivalries
- Building NXT from a developmental brand into a critically praised product is his most complete professional achievement
- The Pedigree is wrestling's most effective 'makes you look unconscious' finishing sequence
