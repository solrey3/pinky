# Billy Beane

Former professional baseball player and general manager of the Oakland Athletics, central figure of Michael Lewis's *Moneyball*. Beane transformed baseball by replacing traditional scouting with sabermetric analysis, proving that a small-market team could compete with wealthy franchises through intellectual capital rather than financial capital.
