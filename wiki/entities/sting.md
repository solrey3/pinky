# Sting

#9 all-time.

**Real Name:** Steve Borden
**Born:** 1959, Omaha NE
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1985–2015
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #9 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#9 all-time. The most loyal franchise player in wrestling history — he spent his entire WCW career as that company's face, never going to WWF/WWE during the Monday Night Wars when every other star crossed over. His NWO Wolfpac Sting character (mysterious, silent, watching from the rafters) is one of the most effective long-term character turns ever executed.

## Highlights

- Multiple WCW/TNA World Champion; AEW TNT Champion
- The Crow Sting character (1996–1998) set a new standard for long-term character development
- His 1997 WCW Starrcade match vs. Hogan was WCW's most anticipated main event — and it was botched by a fast count controversy
- His TNA run extended his career credibility well past the WCW era
- His final match against Darby Allin at AEW Revolution 2024 was a perfect retirement
