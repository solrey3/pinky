# Rusev (Miro)

#55.

**Real Name:** Miroslav Barnyashev
**Born:** 1984, Plovdiv, Bulgaria
**Nationality:** Bulgarian
**Active:** 2006–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #55 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#55. The most underutilized main event talent in wrestling. Rusev's Bulgarian Brute character — built on genuine Olympic weightlifting credentials and a methodical physical style — was over enough to sustain a year-long undefeated streak in WWE (2014–2015). His marriage to Lana (CJ Perry) was turned into a WWE storyline and then into an AEW relationship. His AEW TNT Championship run showed his full range.

## Highlights

- WWE US Champion; AEW TNT Champion
- His undefeated streak (2014–2015) was WWE's best booking of a monster heel in a decade
- His AEW TNT Championship run ('God's Favorite Champion') was his best character and match work
- The Accolade (Camel Clutch variant) is among wrestling's most convincing submission holds
- His legitimate Bulgarian Olympic weightlifting credentials gave his character authentic physical credibility
