# Prince

American singer, songwriter, musician, and record producer. Widely regarded as one of the greatest musicians of his generation.

## Albums

- [Sign o' the Times (1987)](../music/albums/sign-o-the-times.md) — Ninth studio album; widely regarded as Prince's best album. Ranked #24 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.

## Posthumous Releases

- **'Timeless'** (announced June 4, 2026) — New album collecting previously unreleased songs from Prince's vault. Via BrooklynVegan. Prince died in 2016 and left behind a reported vault of hundreds of unfinished and unreleased recordings. 'Timeless' is the latest attempt by his estate to surface that material.
