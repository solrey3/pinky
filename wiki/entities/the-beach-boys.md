# The Beach Boys

American rock band formed in Hawthorne, California in 1961. The group's original lineup consisted of brothers Brian, Dennis, and Carl Wilson, their cousin Mike Love, and friend Al Jardine.

## Albums

- [Pet Sounds (1966)](../music/albums/pet-sounds.md) — Eleventh studio album; widely regarded as one of the greatest albums ever made. Ranked #25 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
