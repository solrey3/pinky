# Iga Swiatek

Polish WTA No. 1 and four-time Roland Garros champion (2020, 2022, 2023, 2024). The most dominant clay-court player of her era, with a record at Roland Garros that rivals all-time greats. Known for a heavy topspin forehand and near-mechanical consistency; she has turned the French Open into what Nadal's run looked like for men. In May 2026, she was **eliminated at Roland Garros Round 4** by Marta Kostyuk (15-seed) 7-5, 6-1 — the biggest upset of the 2026 women's draw and the most significant Roland Garros loss of her career to date.

## 2026 Roland Garros

- **R4:** Lost to Marta Kostyuk (15) — 5-7, 1-6 (from Swiatek's perspective: 7-5, 6-1 for Kostyuk)
- The loss ended her bid for a fifth Roland Garros title and removed the defending champion from the draw.
- Cross-reference: Gauff (4-seed) had also been upset earlier in the tournament (by Potapova, 28-seed) — this Roland Garros is historically volatile in the women's draw.
