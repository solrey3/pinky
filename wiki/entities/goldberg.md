# Goldberg

#45.

**Real Name:** Bill Goldberg
**Born:** 1966, Tulsa OK
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1997–2004, 2016–2019, 2022
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / football player
**Personal Rank:** #45 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#45. The greatest streak in wrestling history. Goldberg's 173-0 undefeated run in WCW (1997–1998) built one of the most effective wrestling mythologies ever created — a legitimate former NFL player who hit the ring, speared people through barricades, and jackhammered them into the mat. When Kevin Nash ended the streak, it was WCW's most significant booking decision (and their most debated).

## Highlights

- WCW World Champion; multiple stints as a major draw
- The 173-0 streak is the most effective undefeated character run in wrestling, executed cleanly
- His WCW championship win over Hogan at Atlanta Nitro (1998) drew a genuine 40,000-person stadium crowd
- His spear and jackhammer are two of the most convincing power moves in wrestling
- His late-career WWE run (vs. Lesnar, vs. Owens) proved his drawing power survived time
