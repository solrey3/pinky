# Shai Gilgeous-Alexander

The 2026 NBA MVP and the best player in the Western Conference — and the central figure in the 2026 WCF elimination. The Oklahoma City Thunder were eliminated in Game 7 (Spurs 111, Thunder 103) despite SGA being the league's best player entering the series. The "taint" framing (noted by Zach Lowe) applies: OKC went into Game 7 without Jalen Williams and Ajay Mitchell. SGA was held in check in the decisive stretches.

**Born:** 1998, Hamilton ON
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Position:** SG/PG
**Active:** 2018–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The 2026 NBA MVP and the best player in the Western Conference. SGA's scoring versatility — floaters, pull-up jumpers, Euro-steps — combined with his defensive presence makes him the most complete player of his age. His Oklahoma City Thunder have become the NBA's best team around his development, the most sustainable young team building in a decade.

## Highlights

- 2026 NBA MVP; multiple All-NBA First Team selections
- The most consistent scorer in the NBA by efficiency metrics (2024–2026)
- His OKC Thunder have the best young supporting cast in the NBA
- His development from a secondary piece (Clippers) to the league's best player took fewer years than any recent superstar
- The Canadian basketball pipeline (after Nash and Wiggins) has its most complete star
