# Gary Payton

The Glove.

**Born:** 1968, Oakland CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 1990–2007
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The Glove. The greatest defensive point guard in NBA history. Nine All-Defensive First Team selections. One championship (2006 Heat). His trash talk was so effective it functionally constituted a physical defensive weapon. His 1996 NBA Finals game (holding Michael Jordan to one of his least efficient Finals performances) is the definitive individual defensive series in Finals history.

## Highlights

- 9x All-Defensive First Team — the most of any guard in NBA history
- 1x Defensive Player of the Year (1996)
- 1x NBA Champion (2006 Heat, as a veteran complementary player)
- His trash talk is basketball's most developed psycho-warfare tool — he spoke constantly and specifically
- His 1996 Finals defense on Michael Jordan is the best single-series defensive performance in NBA history
