# PAC

#59.

**Real Name:** Benjamin Satterly
**Born:** 1985, Newcastle, England
**Nationality:** British
**Active:** 2006–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #59 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#59. The best aerial wrestler in professional wrestling. PAC's precision on high-risk moves — the Black Arrow (corkscrew shooting star press), the SSP, the Spanish Fly — is unmatched. His Dragon Gate run (2010–2013) built a reputation for perfection in the air; his WWE NXT Cruiserweight run reinforced it. His AEW run has made him one of the division's most credible champions.

## Highlights

- Dragon Gate champion; WWE Cruiserweight Champion; AEW All-Atlantic Champion
- The Black Arrow (corkscrew SSP) is the most precisely executed high-risk move in professional wrestling
- His WWE Cruiserweight championship run (2016–2017) legitimized 205 Live during its good period
- His AEW matches with Kenny Omega and Chris Jericho show he can work heavyweight styles despite his size
- His real name is Ben Satterly — PAC the character is entirely a performance creation, which is the point
