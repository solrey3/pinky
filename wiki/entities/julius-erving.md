# Julius Erving

The inventor of above-the-rim basketball.

**Born:** 1950, East Meadow NY
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF
**Active:** 1971–1987
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 4 (#17)

## Summary

The inventor of above-the-rim basketball. Dr. J's ABA career (three championships, four MVPs) is the foundation of his legacy; his NBA career (one championship, one MVP) is the confirmation. His 1980 Finals layup — going under the backboard from the left side and somehow laying it in — is the most beautiful single play in basketball history.

## Highlights

- 1x NBA Champion (1983); 1x NBA MVP (1981); 3x ABA Champion; 4x ABA MVP
- His 1980 Finals baseline layup is the most aesthetically perfect play in basketball history
- His ABA career (1971–1976) built the stylistic vocabulary of the modern above-the-rim game
- 5x NBA All-Star; 11x ABA All-Star — his combined statistical career is incomparable
- He was the first player who made the game look like art rather than just athletics
