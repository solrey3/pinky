# Radiohead

English rock band formed in Abingdon, Oxfordshire, in 1985. Led by Thom Yorke, the band is known for their experimental approach and evolution across genres.

## Albums

- [The Bends (1995)](../music/albums/the-bends.md) — Second studio album; marked their shift from grunge to expansive guitar rock. Ranked #9 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
