# Brock Lesnar (The Beast Incarnate)

#33.

**Real Name:** Brock Lesnar
**Born:** 1977, Webster SD
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2002–2004, 2012–2024
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / UFC heavyweight champion
**Personal Rank:** #33 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#33. The most physically freakish athlete in wrestling history — 290 lbs of legitimate muscle who was an NCAA Division I wrestling champion and won the UFC heavyweight championship in his seventh professional fight. His matches benefit from the genuine aura that his athletic credentials provide. His 2004 retirement at 26 to pursue football and UFC, and his subsequent returns, give his appearances genuine scarcity value.

## Highlights

- Multiple WWE/Universal Heavyweight Champion; UFC Heavyweight Champion
- NCAA Division I wrestling champion (Minnesota, 2000)
- UFC Heavyweight Champion (2008–2010) — one of the greatest sports crossovers in history
- His WM19 shooting star press attempt (and concussion landing) is the bravest botch in wrestling
- Ending Undertaker's 21-0 WrestleMania streak at WM30 is the most shocking finish in WM history
