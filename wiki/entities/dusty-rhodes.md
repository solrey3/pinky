# Dusty Rhodes

#46.

**Real Name:** Virgil Runnels
**Born:** 1945, Austin TX
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1967–2012
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #46 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#46. The American Dream — the most beloved babyface in NWA history and the character who convinced working-class wrestling fans that one of their own could be a world champion. His promos ('hard times, baby') were the most emotionally resonant in NWA history. As WWE's head of talent development (NXT's creator), he built the infrastructure that produced Rollins, Reigns, and Ambrose.

## Highlights

- Multiple NWA World Heavyweight Champion
- 'Hard Times' promo (1985) against Ric Flair is the greatest babyface promo in wrestling history
- His feud with Ric Flair across the NWA territories is one of the longest and most beloved feuds in wrestling history
- As NXT's creative lead (2012–2015), he built the blueprint for modern developmental wrestling
- The polka-dot gimmick forced on him by Vince McMahon was a deliberate effort to humiliate a WCW/NWA star — and he made it work anyway
