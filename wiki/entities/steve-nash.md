# Steve Nash

The greatest shooting point guard in NBA history.

**Born:** 1974, Victoria BC
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 1996–2014
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The greatest shooting point guard in NBA history. Two consecutive MVPs (2005, 2006) while leading the Phoenix Suns' Seven Seconds or Less offense — a system that revolutionized NBA basketball by showing that pace, passing, and three-point shooting could work at the highest level. His 90.4% career free throw percentage is the highest among multiple full-season players.

## Highlights

- 2x NBA MVP (2005, 2006); 8x All-Star; 5x assists champion
- His Seven Seconds or Less Phoenix offense (2004–2010) invented the modern pace-and-space NBA
- 90.4% career free throw percentage — the highest in NBA history (among qualified attempts)
- Canadian basketball's greatest ambassador before Shai Gilgeous-Alexander
- His soccer training background is the only confirmed case of a non-basketball sport improving NBA footwork at the elite level
