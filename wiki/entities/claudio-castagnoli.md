# Cesaro / Claudio Castagnoli

#56.

**Real Name:** Claudio Castagnoli
**Born:** 1980, Lucerne, Switzerland
**Nationality:** Swiss
**Active:** 2002–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #56 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#56. The most physically impressive wrestler who never held a world title. The Swiss Superman's running European uppercut from the ring apron into a standing opponent is the most replicated spot in modern wrestling. His strength feats (the Cesaro Swing, the pop-up uppercut) are genuine showcases. He and Sheamus had the best tag team run in WWE in the 2010s. He is currently the Blackpool Combat Club's most technically credible member.

## Highlights

- Tag Team Champion (WWE, multiple); ROH World Champion
- The Cesaro Swing — 30+ rotations of a grown man while holding his legs — is wrestling's most exhausting-to-watch spot
- His match with Bryan Danielson at NXT TakeOver 2015 is one of the best TV matches of that year
- The Bar tag team with Sheamus is the best legitimate pure wrestling tag team of 2010s WWE
- His BCC work with Moxley and Wheeler Yuta shows his post-WWE creative fulfillment
