# Andrade / Andrade El Idolo

#75.

**Real Name:** Manuel Andrade
**Born:** 1989, Gómez Palacio, Mexico
**Nationality:** Mexican
**Active:** 2007–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #75 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#75. The most criminally underutilized talent in wrestling. Andrade's NXT Championship run (2018–2019) included some of the best TV matches in NXT history; his main roster WWE career was a sustained misuse; his AEW run has been inconsistent but his match quality remains as high as anyone's. His matches with Johnny Gargano (NXT 2019) and Aleister Black/Malakai Black are canonical.

## Highlights

- NXT Champion; ROH World Champion; CMLL World Welterweight Champion
- His NXT Championship match with Aleister Black (NXT TakeOver Phoenix, 2019) is ★★★★½ — the best NXT match of that year
- His CMLL career (2008–2015) was the best in Mexico during that period
- His technical style is genuinely Mexico-trained — fast, fluid, and built on genuine lucha fundamentals
- His wife (Charlotte Flair) and father-in-law (Ric Flair) connect him to wrestling's most prominent family
