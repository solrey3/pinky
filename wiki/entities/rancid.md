# Rancid

American punk rock band formed in Berkeley, California in 1991. Founded by Tim Armstrong and Matt Freeman, the band is known for their ska-punk sound.

## Albums

- […And Out Come the Wolves (1995)](../music/albums/and-out-come-the-wolves.md) — Third studio album; helped revive mainstream interest in punk rock. Certified platinum. Ranked #17 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
