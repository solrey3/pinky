# The Strokes

American rock band formed in New York City in 1998. Led by Julian Casablancas, they were a leading band in the early-2000s garage rock revival.

## Albums

- [Is This It (2001)](../music/albums/is-this-it.md) — Debut studio album; widely considered one of the most influential albums of all time. Ranked #32 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
