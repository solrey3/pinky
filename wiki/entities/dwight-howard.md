# Dwight Howard

Superman.

**Born:** 1985, Atlanta GA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** C
**Active:** 2004–2022
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

Superman. The most dominant defensive center of his era. Three consecutive Defensive Player of the Year awards (2009–2011), one Finals appearance (2009 Orlando Magic), one championship (2020 Lakers). His inability to develop a reliable offensive game limited his overall impact despite being physically as gifted as Shaquille O'Neal.

## Highlights

- 3x consecutive Defensive Player of the Year (2009–2011)
- 1x NBA Champion (2020 Los Angeles Lakers)
- 8x All-Star; led the league in rebounds seven times
- His 2009 Orlando Magic (taking a team with no other legitimate All-Stars to the Finals) is the greatest individual defensive carrying performance of his era
- His free throw shooting (56.8% career) is the most significant skill gap between potential and realization in his generation
