# Randy Orton

#34.

**Real Name:** Randall Orton
**Born:** 1980, Knoxville TN
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2000–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #34 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#34. The most gifted second-generation wrestler in history — son of 'Cowboy' Bob Orton. His Legend Killer character at 24 was one of the best heel gimmicks in WWE history. The RKO outta nowhere meme is wrestling's most viral spot. His methodical, slow-burning heel work in the 2009–2011 period (with The Legacy) is criminally underrated.

## Highlights

- 14x World Champion (tied with Triple H for third-most WWE title reigns)
- The RKO counter (catching any opponent mid-move) has produced wrestling's most viral clips
- His heel run (2009) with The Legacy and his match with Triple H at Backlash 2009 is his best work
- The Punt Kick was banned multiple times for being too convincing — which is a testament to his selling and his opponent's
- His 2023 return from a serious back injury (spinal fusion) to WM39 is one of the best medical return stories
