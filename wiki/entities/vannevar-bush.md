# Vannevar Bush

American engineer and science administrator. In his 1945 essay "As We May Think," he proposed the Memex — a conceptual personal knowledge device for storing and linking documents via associative trails. Widely regarded as a conceptual ancestor of hypertext and the World Wide Web.
