# Mercedes Moné

**Real Name:** Mercedes Varnado  
**Ring Name(s):** Mercedes Moné (AEW/NJPW); Sasha Banks (WWE)  
**Born:** 1992, Fairfield, CA  
**Nationality:** American  
**Active:** 2010–present

## Summary

One of the most decorated women's wrestlers of her generation. As **Sasha Banks** in WWE, she was a multiple-time Women's Champion and part of the "Four Horsewomen" faction alongside Charlotte Flair, Becky Lynch, and Bayley — the group credited with legitimizing the WWE women's division in the mid-2010s. Her in-ring ability is elite by any era's standard: technically precise, bump-taking without hesitation, and with a character (The Boss) that translated on the main roster.

She departed WWE in 2022 following a widely reported disagreement over creative direction, subsequently working for **NJPW/STARDOM** where she operated as **The CEO** and built an international brand outside the WWE umbrella. She debuted in AEW and established herself as a top women's attraction.

On **June 3, 2026 (AEW Dynamite — Summer Blockbuster)**, Moné returned to AEW via the **Women's Owen Hart Tournament Wild Card slot**, defeating Alex Windsor and advancing in the women's bracket. Her return is the most significant women's roster development of the AEW summer season — she arrives with a full international résumé and into a tournament structure designed to deliver major women's matchups. AEW Forbidden Door is the implied event where her arc builds toward.
