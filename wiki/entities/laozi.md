# Laozi

Legendary Chinese philosopher and author of the *Tao Te Ching*, traditionally dated to the 6th century BCE. His name means "Old Master." Whether he was a historical figure or a composite is debated, but the *Tao Te Ching* remains one of the most translated and influential texts in world philosophy. Its teachings on *wu wei* (effortless action), simplicity, and yielding have shaped Taoism, Zen Buddhism, and modern contemplative practice.
