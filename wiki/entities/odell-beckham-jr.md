# Odell Beckham Jr.

**Position:** Wide Receiver  
**Born:** 1992, Baton Rouge, LA  
**Nationality:** American  
**Active:** 2014–present  
**Teams:** New York Giants (2014–2018, 2026–), Cleveland Browns, Los Angeles Rams

## Summary

One of the most electrifying wide receivers of his era, OBJ made his name with the New York Giants — most memorably with the one-handed catch on November 23, 2014 against Dallas, considered the greatest single catch in NFL history. He is as well known for his personality and cultural visibility off the field as his production on it. After stints in Cleveland and a Super Bowl ring with the Rams (Super Bowl LVI, Feb 2022), he returned to free agency and has bounced between injury recovery and market timing.

On **June 2, 2026**, per NFL insider Mike Garafolo and Ian Rapoport (RapSheet), the Giants are **signing OBJ** after a morning workout in East Rutherford. The original franchise — the team that turned him into a star — brings him back. NYC Mayor Zuberi Mamdani's bedtime-repeal order for the Knicks Finals run dropped the same morning; OBJ's signing dropped the same news cycle. June came in swinging.
