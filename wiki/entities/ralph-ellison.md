# Ralph Ellison

American novelist and literary critic (1914–1994), best known for *Invisible Man* (1952), which won the National Book Award. Ellison was deeply influenced by jazz, blues, and modernist literature. His work explores Black identity, American democracy, and the individual's search for authentic selfhood within oppressive structures. He also wrote influential essays on literature, music, and culture.
