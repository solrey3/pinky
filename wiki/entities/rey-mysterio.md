# Rey Mysterio Jr.

#27.

**Real Name:** Óscar Gutierrez
**Born:** 1974, San Diego CA
**Nationality:** Mexican-American
**Active:** 1989–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #27 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#27. The greatest cruiserweight in wrestling history. Rey Mysterio's WWE run (2002–present) proved that a 5'6", 175-pound wrestler could be a world champion in a company built around giants. His lucha libre style — the 619, the hurricanrana, the springboard cross-body — was entirely new to American wrestling audiences when he arrived. His mask is wrestling's most iconic accessory.

## Highlights

- WWE World Heavyweight Champion; multiple IC and US titles
- WCW Cruiserweight run (1996–1999) introduced lucha libre to American cable television
- His match with Eddie Guerrero at Halloween Havoc 1997 is the best cruiserweight match in American television history
- His WM22 Royal Rumble win (dedicated to Eddie Guerrero, who had died) was the most emotional Royal Rumble result ever
- His son Dominik becoming a wrestler and then turning heel on him created a real-life story WWE capitalized on
