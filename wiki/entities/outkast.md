# Outkast

American hip hop duo formed in East Point, Georgia, in 1992. Composed of rappers André 3000 and Big Boi, the duo is regarded as one of the greatest hip hop acts of all time.

## Albums

- [Aquemini (1998)](../music/albums/aquemini.md) — Third studio album; blends live instrumentation with funk, soul, and rock. Double platinum. Ranked #14 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
