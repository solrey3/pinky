# Cody Rhodes (The American Nightmare)

#15.

**Real Name:** Cody Rhodes
**Born:** 1985, Marietta GA
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 2006–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #15 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#15. The most complete story in wrestling — son of Dusty Rhodes who was released by WWE as a mid-card act, built AEW from the ground up as a founder, returned to WWE, and won the Undisputed Championship at WM40 to complete his father's unfinished dream. The 'Finish the Story' arc (2022–2024) is the most emotionally resonant long-term wrestling narrative in 20 years.

## Highlights

- Undisputed WWE Champion (WM40, 2024)
- Co-founded AEW in 2019 with the Elite and Tony Khan — built an alternative to WWE from nothing
- His TNT Championship Open Challenge run was the best recurring TV wrestling feature of 2020–2021
- Beating Roman Reigns at WM40 to 'finish the story' was the payoff of a two-year narrative built entirely around his father's legacy
- The American Nightmare character is a babyface who believes in himself without apology
