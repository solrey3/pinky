# Oasis

English rock band formed in Manchester in 1991. Led by Liam Gallagher and Noel Gallagher, they were one of the most successful bands of the Britpop era.

## Albums

- [Definitely Maybe (1994)](../music/albums/definitely-maybe.md) — Debut studio album; fastest-selling debut album in British music history at the time. Ranked #38 on SolReyDio's Top 40ish.
