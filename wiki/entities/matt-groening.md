# Matt Groening

Creator of The Simpsons (1989–present) and Futurama (1999–2023).

**Born:** 1954, Portland OR
**Nationality:** American
**Active:** 1978–present
**Profession:** Cartoonist, television writer, producer

## Summary

Creator of The Simpsons (1989–present) and Futurama (1999–2023). Groening's Life in Hell comic strip was the source from which The Simpsons sprang, but The Simpsons transcended its origin to become the defining American animated sitcom and the longest-running American primetime scripted television series.

## Highlights

- The Simpsons (1989–present) — #1 personal TV ranking; the defining cultural text of 1990s America
- Futurama (1999–2023) — the most scientifically rigorous animated comedy ever produced
- Life in Hell (comic strip, 1977–2012) — the predecessor that developed his visual and satirical vocabulary
- His Simpsons visual design (yellow skin, four fingers, overbite) is the most recognized character design in animation history
- His creation of Springfield as a geographically undefined American city is the most useful fictional setting in television
