# David Sklansky

American poker player, mathematician, and author of foundational poker strategy texts. *The Theory of Poker* (1987) established the mathematical and strategic principles underlying all poker variations. Sklansky is known for his analytical rigor and his insistence that expected value, not results, should drive every decision.
