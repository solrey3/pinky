# Kyle Fletcher

#63.

**Real Name:** Kyle Fletcher
**Born:** 1997, Coffs Harbour, Australia
**Nationality:** Australian
**Active:** 2015–present
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Personal Rank:** #63 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#63. The best young heavyweight in AEW. Fletcher's development from tag team specialist (Aussie Open, with Mark Davis) to legitimate heavyweight singles main event contender has been one of AEW's most complete developmental stories. His technical ability at 27 already rivals his predecessors'. His matches with Will Ospreay and Konosuke Takeshita show he can work at the highest level available.

## Highlights

- AEW International Champion; NJPW STRONG Champion
- His tag team Aussie Open (with Mark Davis) is the best Australian tag team in wrestling history
- His singles matches with Will Ospreay in AEW are among the best technical exchanges of 2024
- The strong style he has developed is appropriate for his physical build — compact, hard-hitting
- His Don Callis Family alignment gave him a heel credential that his natural charisma can subvert
