# Alexandre Dumas

French writer of the Romantic period, best known for his historical adventure novels. *The Three Musketeers* (1844) contains direct references to *Don Quixote*, attesting to its pervasive influence on subsequent Western literature.

**Lived:** 1802–1870  
**Notable Works:** *The Three Musketeers*, *The Count of Monte Cristo*
