# Marta Kostyuk

**Nationality:** Ukrainian  
**Born:** 2002, Kyiv  
**WTA Ranking:** 15 (as of May 2026)  
**Active:** 2017–present  
**Professional:** WTA Tour

## Summary

Ukrainian WTA player seeded 15th at the 2026 Roland Garros. Known as a powerful baseliner with a big forehand and a willingness to dictate points from the back of the court. Has been outspoken about the war in Ukraine and drawn attention for refusing to shake hands with Russian and Belarusian opponents as a protest posture. On **May 31, 2026**, she eliminated **Iga Swiatek** — the three-time defending Roland Garros champion and 3-seed — in Round 4, **7-5, 6-1**. The 6-1 second set against the most dominant clay-court player of her generation is one of the most dominant closing sets in a major upset at Roland Garros in recent memory.

## Key Moment

- **2026-05-31 — Roland Garros Round 4:** Defeats Iga Swiatek (3-seed, defending champion) 7-5, 6-1. The first set was competitive; the second was a dismantling. Swiatek's three-year reign at Roland Garros ends, and the women's draw enters the quarterfinals without its two biggest favorites (Gauff was upset earlier by Potapova). Kostyuk is a legitimate quarterfinalist.
