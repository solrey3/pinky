# Kobe Bryant

The most technically complete shooting guard in NBA history and the closest approximation of Michael Jordan in playing style.

**Born:** 1978, Philadelphia PA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 1996–2016
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 2 (#5)

## Summary

The most technically complete shooting guard in NBA history and the closest approximation of Michael Jordan in playing style. Five championships with the Lakers, two Finals MVPs, one regular season MVP. His 81-point game against the Raptors (2006) is the second-highest single-game scoring total in NBA history. His Mamba Mentality is a complete philosophy of competitive excellence.

## Highlights

- 5x NBA Champion; 2x Finals MVP; 1x regular-season MVP
- 81 points vs. Toronto Raptors (January 22, 2006) — second only to Wilt's 100
- 4x NBA scoring champion; 18x All-Star selection
- His technical skill set — post game, mid-range, off-the-dribble — is the most complete of any wing in NBA history
- His death in a helicopter crash (January 26, 2020) ended a post-playing career that was becoming as accomplished as his playing career
