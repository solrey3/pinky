# Shaquille O'Neal

The most physically dominant player in NBA history.

**Born:** 1972, Newark NJ
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** C
**Active:** 1992–2011
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 3 (#10)

## Summary

The most physically dominant player in NBA history. Shaq's combination of size (7'1", 325 lbs of functional muscle), athleticism, and skill was never seen before and has not been seen since. Four championships, three consecutive Finals MVPs. The 2001 Shaq — playing at near-perfect efficiency — is the greatest individual season peak in center history.

## Highlights

- 4x NBA Champion (2000, 2001, 2002 Lakers; 2006 Heat); 3x Finals MVP
- His 2001 season (28.7 ppg, 12.7 rpg, 58.8 FG%) is the most physically dominant season in NBA history
- 15x All-Star; 3x scoring champion; 1x regular-season MVP
- The only player in NBA history who could average 30+ points and 15+ rebounds while shooting 58%+ from the field
- His free throw shooting (52.7% career) is the most significant skill gap between his ability and his ceiling — and he still won four titles
