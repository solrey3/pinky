# Vince Carter

Vinsanity.

**Born:** 1977, Daytona Beach FL
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF/SG
**Active:** 1998–2020
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

Vinsanity. The most exciting dunker in NBA history and the player who popularized dunking as a basketball art form. His 2000 Slam Dunk Contest performance is the most complete single dunk contest in history. His 22-year career is the longest in NBA history. His dunk over 7'2" French player Frédéric Weis at the 2000 Olympics ('Le dunk de la mort') is the most famous international basketball moment.

## Highlights

- 1998 Rookie of the Year; 8x All-Star
- His 2000 Slam Dunk Contest is the greatest individual dunking display in basketball history
- Le dunk de la mort (2000 Olympics) — jumping over a 7'2" player — is the most spectacular play in international basketball
- His 22-year career (1998–2020) is the longest in NBA history
- His Toronto career (1998–2004) launched basketball into Canadian consciousness permanently
