# Ralph Waldo Emerson

American essayist, lecturer, philosopher, and poet (1803–1882), the central figure of Transcendentalism. His essays — particularly "Self-Reliance," "The American Scholar," and "Nature" — championed individual intuition over institutional authority and helped define American intellectual identity. A former Unitarian minister, he broke with organized religion to pursue a more direct, personal spirituality.
