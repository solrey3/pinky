# Michael Lewis

American author and financial journalist known for turning complex economic and statistical stories into compelling narratives. His books — including *Liar's Poker*, *Moneyball*, *The Blind Side*, *The Big Short*, and *Flash Boys* — consistently find market inefficiencies, institutional blind spots, and the human stories behind data-driven revolutions.
