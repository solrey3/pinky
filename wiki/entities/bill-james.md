# Bill James

American baseball writer and statistician, the pioneer of sabermetrics. Working as a night watchman in Kansas, James self-published the *Baseball Abstract* series, revolutionizing how the sport analyzed player performance. His work was initially ignored by professional baseball but eventually transformed the industry.
