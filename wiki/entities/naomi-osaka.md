# Naomi Osaka

**Nationality:** Japanese (born in Japan; raised in USA)  
**Born:** 1997, Chūō, Osaka  
**WTA Ranking:** ~16 (as of May 2026)  
**Active:** 2013–present (with maternity leave 2022–2023)  
**Grand Slams:** Australian Open 2019, 2021; US Open 2018, 2020

## Summary

Four-time Grand Slam champion and one of the most recognized athletes in the world. A powerful baseliner who plays her best tennis on hard courts — her four Slams are all on hard surfaces. Osaka's career has been defined as much by her mental health advocacy and public battles with anxiety and depression as by her on-court results. She took an extended absence in 2022 and returned in 2023 after giving birth to her daughter.

Her 2026 Roland Garros run reached **Round 4 as the 16-seed** before she was eliminated by world No. 1 **Aryna Sabalenka 7-5, 6-3** on June 2, 2026. The loss is notable context: Osaka was previously eliminated at Roland Garros R4 in the prior year by Swiatek (7-5, 6-3 was also the score in 2025); clay remains the surface where the gap between her and the WTA elite is most visible. Her seeding at 16 reflects a return to the top tier of the game post-maternity.
