# Evening Brief — Sunday, May 31, 2026

**Author:** The Evening Brief

## Summary

Sunday's evening dispatch is one of the most event-dense single days of 2026. The San Antonio Spurs closed Game 7 against the Oklahoma City Thunder 111–103, reaching the NBA Finals for the first time in over 20 years. Victor Wembanyama, 22 years old, was visibly overcome with emotion at the buzzer. The Spurs will meet the New York Knicks in the Finals — Game 1: June 3, 8:30 PM EDT, ABC. OKC's season ends, again short-handed after Jalen Williams and Ajay Mitchell missed Game 7.

Markets closed with all four major indices within 1–2% of their 52-week highs simultaneously. AMD surged +14.79% to $516, powering the Nasdaq above 26,900 while NVDA fell 3.81% — the AI chip rotation from Nvidia to AMD is now a multi-week story. WTI crude collapsed 6.95% ($87.36) as the White House floated a nuclear-free Iran counterproposal and Kevin Hassett put a "month or two" timeline on the Strait of Hormuz reopening. Prediction markets are pricing near-certain resolution.

WWE Clash in Italy ran in Turin with a stacked card: Cody Rhodes retained the WWE Championship over Gunther in a controversial finish; Roman Reigns retained the World Heavyweight Championship over Jacob Fatu in Tribal Combat; Rhea Ripley retained the Women's title with Charlotte Flair interference; **Sol Ruca** defeated Becky Lynch to win the Women's Intercontinental Championship; Brock Lesnar defeated Oba Femi in a WrestleMania 42 rematch that required seven F-5s. Kevin Owens posted "I miss wrestling" on X. King of the Ring and Queen of the Ring 2026 brackets were revealed. Cody Rhodes told TMZ he wants a match on a decommissioned aircraft carrier.

Tennis: Iga Swiatek, the defending Roland Garros champion and 3-seed, was eliminated in Round 4 by Marta Kostyuk (15-seed) 7-5, 6-1 — the biggest upset of the women's draw. Zverev, Auger-Aliassime, and Mensik advanced on the men's side. Golf: Celine Boutier wins the LPGA ShopRite Classic in Galloway, NJ. Charles Schwab Challenge at Colonial ends with Russell Henley and Eric Cole tied at -12, forcing a playoff. Chess: Magnus Carlsen hit 5000 ELO at Norway Chess 2026, defeating tournament leader Firouzja in Round 6.

Tech: a 17-million-device botnet dismantled by Dutch police and the NCSC. Cloudflare Turnstile fingerprinting controversy (WebGL collection flagged by researchers). ChatGPT for Google Sheets demonstrated to exfiltrate full workbook contents. A new SSD side-channel attack infers browsing behavior from write-timing patterns. Creatine study: 30% reduction in Alzheimer's-related cognitive decline markers — the gym staple has neuroprotective implications. NVIDIA revealed it will announce its first consumer CPU in over a decade at Computex 2026. European Commission fined Temu €200M under the Digital Services Act.
