**Capture Date:** 2026-05-25  
**Source:** [raw/journal/movie-rankings.md](../raw/journal/movie-rankings.md)

## Summary

Personal all-time movie rankings — 67 films listed, all tied at rank 1 (indicating a non-hierarchical list of favorites rather than a strict ordered ranking). The list skews heavily toward comedies, crime films, and genre-bending classics. Heavy representation from the 1970s–1990s golden era. Notable international inclusions: *City of God* (Brazil), *Hard Boiled* (Hong Kong), *Akira* (Japan). The Kubrick index is high: *Full Metal Jacket*, *A Clockwork Orange*, *Dr. Strangelove*, *2001: A Space Odyssey*.

Top-cited directors by implication: Kubrick, Mel Brooks, Woody Allen, the Coen Brothers, Wes Anderson, Christopher Nolan, Takeshi Kitano (adjacent via *Bloodsport*-era action). Comedy dominates — the first film listed, *Spaceballs*, sets the tone: irreverent, subversive, self-aware.

## Key Observations

- **Comedies dominate the top:** Spaceballs, Full Metal Jacket (satirical), Coming to America, Groundhog Day, Brewster's Millions, The Princess Bride, Blazing Saddles, When Harry Met Sally, Airplane!, Zoolander — laughter as a primary film value
- **Genre diversity:** Military satire (Full Metal Jacket, Dr. Strangelove), martial arts (Enter the Dragon, Bloodsport, Hard Boiled), anime (Akira), crime drama (Rounders, Glengarry Glen Ross, Fargo), sci-fi (The Matrix, 2001, Pi, WarGames), psychological thriller (Fight Club, Memento, Nightcrawler, Requiem for a Dream)
- **Poker/gambling sub-canon:** Rounders, The Harder They Come (indirectly), Searching for Bobby Fischer
- **NJ/NY cultural touchstones:** Ferris Bueller's Day Off, Six Degrees of Separation, Do the Right Thing, Boogie Nights, Adaptation
- **Music film notable inclusion:** 24 Hour Party People (Joy Division / Factory Records), Thirty Two Short Films About Glenn Gould
- **Only one animated film:** Akira — not Disney, not Pixar
- **The Harder They Come** appears on both the movie list and the SolReyDio album canon — the soundtrack is its own canonical entry

## Synthesis Notes

This list parallels the SolReyDio albums canon in structure — "arbitrary and reductive" ranking that functions as a self-portrait. The equal-ranking approach (all at "1") is more honest than a fake hierarchy; it acknowledges that taste comparison at this level is meaningless. The Kubrick cluster (*Full Metal Jacket*, *Dr. Strangelove*, *A Clockwork Orange*, *2001*) signals a preference for intellectually violent, technically precise filmmaking. The comedy cluster (Spaceballs, Blazing Saddles, Airplane!, Coming to America) signals a deep value for subversive wit.
