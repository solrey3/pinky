# Surveillance Pricing

Surveillance pricing is personalized or targeted pricing that uses observed behavior, identity, location, purchase history, device data, loyalty profiles, or inferred willingness to pay to vary prices. The governance problem is transparency: consumers often cannot tell whether a price reflects supply/demand, ordinary promotion, or individualized data extraction.
