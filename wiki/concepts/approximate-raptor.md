# Approximate RAPTOR

Historical variant of [[RAPTOR]] that estimates player ratings for seasons before 2013-14, extending back to 1976-77 (the first post-ABA/NBA merger season). Because player tracking and play-by-play data were unavailable, it requires different tricks: position adjustments (boosting wings, penalizing centers), heavier reliance on team offensive/defensive ratings, and for seasons from 2000-01 onward, [[Real Plus Minus|RPM]] as an input.

It raises a philosophical question: does it reflect which players were best *in their era given available data*, or which past players would be best *under modern conditions*? Either way, [[Michael Jordan]] dominates all-time WAR and the author concluded his peak was probably slightly higher than [[LeBron James|LeBron]]'s.
