# CARMELO Projections

FiveThirtyEight's previous NBA player projection system, retired when [[RAPTOR]] and [[PREDATOR]] took over. The methodology lives on inside PREDATOR: a three-step process involving (1) regression-based baseline forecast from past ratings plus biographical variables, (2) identification of comparable players, and (3) adjustment based on how those comparables performed relative to their baselines at the same age.

The name CARMELO was a whimsical backronym in the tradition of [[RAPTOR]] and [[DRAYMOND]].
