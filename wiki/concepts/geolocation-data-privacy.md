# Geolocation Data Privacy

Geolocation data privacy is the governance problem around precise location trails generated by phones, vehicles, apps, ad-tech SDKs, and data brokers. The risk is not only government access; commercial sale can expose routines, associations, medical visits, worship, protests, home/work patterns, and other sensitive inferences.
