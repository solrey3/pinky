# Plus-Minus Statistics

A family of basketball metrics that evaluate players by the point differential of their team while they are on the court, adjusted for teammate and opponent quality. The core idea: a player's presence either helps or hurts the team's scoring margin.

Key variants include raw plus-minus, [[Real Adjusted Plus Minus|RAPM]], [[Real Plus Minus|RPM]], [[Box Plus/Minus|BPM]], and [[RAPTOR]]. These statistics assume performance is roughly linear and additive — they do not account for coaching, systems, or teammate synergies.
