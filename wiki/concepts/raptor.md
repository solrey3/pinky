# RAPTOR

Robust Algorithm using Player Tracking and On/Off Ratings — FiveThirtyEight's NBA player evaluation metric. It measures points contributed per 100 possessions relative to a league-average player, combining a box-score component (~85% weight) fitted against long-term RAPM with an on-court/off-court component (~21% weight).

Available from 2013-14 onward (when player tracking data came online). A historical variant, [[Approximate RAPTOR]], extends to 1976-77.
