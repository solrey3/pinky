# Geofence Warrants

A geofence warrant asks a platform or data broker to identify devices that were inside a geographic area during a time window. In the wiki graph, it belongs to the privacy/security boundary around location data, dragnet search, and constitutional limits on bulk digital evidence collection.

## Notes

The operational lesson is provenance and scope. Location trails are sensitive because they reconstruct associations, routines, medical visits, protests, worship, and home/work patterns. Treating geofence warrants as searches forces legal process, minimization, and auditability into a data-access pattern that can otherwise become a generalized dragnet.
