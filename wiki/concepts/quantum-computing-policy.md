# Quantum Computing Policy

Quantum computing policy is the governance layer around quantum research, funding, security, export controls, and strategic infrastructure. It treats quantum capability not only as a science milestone, but as a national-security and economic-competitiveness domain.

## Notes

Quantum policy should be tracked separately from specific technical claims. A policy order can accelerate funding, security requirements, export-control pressure, and agency coordination, but it does not by itself prove progress toward fault-tolerant or useful quantum computation. Link policy moves to technical milestones like [[Quantum Error Correction]] without collapsing one into the other.
