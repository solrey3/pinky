# Pythagorean Expectation

A formula for estimating a team's winning percentage from its points scored and allowed. FiveThirtyEight uses an exponent of 14.3 for the regular season and 13.2 for the playoffs in their [[RAPTOR]]-driven team projections.

After summing individual player ratings weighted by projected minutes, they apply a score-effects multiplier (0.8 regular season, 0.9 playoffs) because good teams often play with a lead, reducing their raw efficiency.
