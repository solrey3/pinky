# PREDATOR

PREDictive rApTOR — the predictive variant of [[RAPTOR]] used by FiveThirtyEight for team and player projections. It uses out-of-sample regression coefficients rather than in-sample ones, making it slightly more robust for forecasting. Correlation with descriptive RAPTOR is extremely high (0.98 offense, 0.95 defense).

Projections add biographical priors (age, height, draft position, awards, college strength) and comparable-player adjustments inherited from the [[CARMELO Projections|CARMELO]] system.
