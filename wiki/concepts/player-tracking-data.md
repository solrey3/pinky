# Player Tracking Data

Detailed spatial and event-level data captured by optical tracking systems (e.g., Second Spectrum) installed in NBA arenas starting in the 2013-14 season. It records player and ball positions, shot locations, defender distances, passing networks, rebounding contests, and more.

[[RAPTOR]] is built exclusively on publicly available tracking and play-by-play data. The availability of this data enabled metrics that move beyond traditional box scores to value spacing, contested shots, and defensive positioning.
