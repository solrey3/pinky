# Replacement Level WAR (NBA)

Wins Above Replacement in basketball, as implemented by [[RAPTOR]], uses a replacement level of -2.75 points per 100 possessions. This threshold was derived from the historical performance of players on two-way contracts — players literally on the fringe between the NBA and the G League.

Unlike many other advanced stats, [[RAPTOR]] uses the same replacement level across all positions. However, replacement-level guards tend to be terrible defensively but tolerable offensively, while replacement-level bigs show the reverse pattern. The WAR multiplier is 0.0005102 for the regular season and 0.0005262 in the playoffs.
