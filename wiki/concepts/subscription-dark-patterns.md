# Subscription Dark Patterns

Subscription dark patterns are product and billing designs that make it easier to enroll than to cancel, obscure recurring costs, hide cancellation paths, or use manipulative retention flows to keep users paying. The governance issue is not simply bad UX; it is whether platforms convert attention, confusion, or friction into recurring revenue against user intent.
