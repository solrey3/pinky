# Bob's Burgers (2011–present)

**Creator:** Loren Bouchard
**Network:** Fox
**Genre:** Animated sitcom
**Category:** Scripted Series
**Rank:** #34 (Scripted Series)

## Summary

Bob Belcher (H. Jon Benjamin) runs a struggling burger restaurant with his wife Linda (John Roberts) and three children: Tina (Dan Mintz), Gene (Eugene Mirman), and Louise (Kristen Schaal). Unlike most animated sitcoms, the Belcher family actually likes and supports each other. The show's warmth is structural, not episodic — every episode ends with the family intact and usually happier.

## Key Themes

- The family that genuinely loves each other as a radical premise in animated TV
- Tina Belcher as a new kind of sitcom heroine: earnest, sexual, obsessed with horses and butts, entirely herself
- Small business survival as a recurring source of gentle tension
- The female characters (Tina, Louise, Linda) are the funniest and most complete in the show
- Warmth as a creative choice: the show refuses to make the Belchers suffer for laughs

## Why It Matters

The best animated family comedy since the golden age of *The Simpsons*. H. Jon Benjamin plays a fundamentally decent man and it works. Tina Belcher is a comedic creation of genuine originality — her romantic fantasy life is rendered with total commitment. Louise Belcher (Kristen Schaal) is one of TV's best child characters. The show won the Emmy for Outstanding Animated Program in 2014. Its consistency across 14 seasons is remarkable.
