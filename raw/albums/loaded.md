# The Velvet Underground — Loaded

**Release Date:** 1970-11-15
**Label:** Cotillion Records
**Producer:** The Velvet Underground, Geoff Haslam

## Overview

Loaded is the fourth studio album by the American rock band the Velvet Underground, released in November 1970 by Atlantic Records subsidiary Cotillion. It was the final album recorded featuring any of the band's original members; lead singer and primary songwriter Lou Reed left the band shortly before the album's release, and guitarist Sterling Morrison and drummer Maureen Tucker left in 1971. For this reason, it is often considered by fans to be the last "true" Velvet Underground album.
