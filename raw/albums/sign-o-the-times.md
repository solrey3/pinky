# Prince — Sign o' the Times

**Release Date:** 1987-03-31
**Label:** Paisley Park / Warner Bros.
**Producer:** Prince

## Overview

Sign o' the Times is the ninth studio album by the American singer, songwriter, producer, and multi-instrumentalist Prince. It was first released on March 31, 1987, as a double album. The album is Prince's first solo album following his disbanding of the Revolution.

Sign o' the Times is a drastic departure from Prince's previous works, taking inspiration from social issues and spirituality. The album's music touches on funk, soul, psychedelic pop, electro, and rock. It reached the top 10 of the Billboard 200 and spawned three top 10 hits on the Billboard Hot 100: the title track, "U Got the Look" and "I Could Never Take the Place of Your Man".

Sign o' the Times is one of the most critically acclaimed albums in popular music, widely being regarded as Prince's best album. It was voted the best album of 1987 in the Pazz & Jop critics poll and was nominated for Album of the Year at the 30th Grammy Awards. It was inducted into the Grammy Hall of Fame in 2017.
