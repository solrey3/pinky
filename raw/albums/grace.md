# Jeff Buckley — Grace

**Release Date:** 1994-08-23
**Label:** Columbia Records
**Producer:** Jeff Buckley, Andy Wallace

## Overview

Grace is the only studio album by the American singer-songwriter Jeff Buckley, released on August 23, 1994 by Columbia Records. After moving from Los Angeles to New York City in 1991, Buckley amassed a following through his performances at Sin-é and signed to Columbia in 1993.

It includes versions of the jazz standard "Lilac Wine", the hymn "Corpus Christi Carol" and the 1984 Leonard Cohen song "Hallelujah". Grace reached number 149 on the US Billboard 200 initially, but after Buckley's death in 1997, its critical standing grew. By 2011, it had achieved sales of two million, and in 2016 it was certified platinum in the US.
