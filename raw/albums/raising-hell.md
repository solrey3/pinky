# Run DMC — Raising Hell

**Release Date:** 1986-05-15

## Wisdom / Themes

SUMMARY
Run DMC presents their 1986 album Raising Hell exploring hip hop music production and cultural breakthrough in detail.

IDEAS:
- Run DMC pioneered rap rock fusion with groundbreaking Aerosmith collaboration on Walk This Way hit. 
- Raising Hell became first rap album achieving multi platinum sales status worldwide commercially. 
- Rick Rubin production style emphasized raw beats and minimalistic powerful drum sounds effectively. 
- Album cover art featured bold bold typography and iconic group poses symbolically. 
- Lyrics addressed social issues like education and street life authentically from experience. 
- My Adidas track sparked major sneaker endorsement deals in music history first. 
- Group dynamic showcased tight rhymes and energetic delivery captivating live audiences everywhere. 
- Release timing aligned perfectly with growing MTV exposure for hip hop acts. 
- Raising Hell influenced countless artists across genres for decades following launch. 
- Jam Master Jay contributions elevated turntablism as essential rap element creatively. 
- Album title referenced hell raising energy matching rebellious youthful spirit accurately.

FACTS:

- First rap album certified multi platinum by RIAA.

- Produced entirely by Rick Rubin at Chung King. 
- Sold over three million copies in United States. 
- Featured pioneering rap rock crossover single hit. 
- Album peaked at number three on Billboard chart. 
- Jam Master Jay handled all turntable scratches. 
- My Adidas led to first rap endorsement deal. 
- Group formed in Hollis Queens New York. 
- Album has six top charting singles released. 
- Influenced development of new jack swing genre. 
- Certified five times platinum in later years. 
- Cover designed by famous artist Steve Carr. 
- Recording took under two weeks total time. 
- Title comes from common phrase used by group. 
- Helped establish Def Jam as major label. 
- Nominated for Grammy award for best rap. 
- Reissued multiple times with bonus tracks added. 
- Cultural landmark in hip hop history timeline.
