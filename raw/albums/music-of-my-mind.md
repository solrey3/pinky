# Stevie Wonder — Music Of My Mind

**Release Date:** 1972-03-03

## Overview

## Wisdom / Themes

SUMMARY
Stevie Wonder presents his 1972 album Music Of My Mind, exploring personal creativity and musical innovation through early Motown artistic expression and self-production experiments.

IDEAS
- Stevie Wonder explores inner thoughts through innovative musical compositions on this album.
- Early independence allowed Stevie Wonder greater creative control over his artistic vision.
- Synthesizers play a prominent role in shaping unique sonic textures throughout.
- Personal experiences deeply influence the lyrical themes presented across multiple tracks.
- Music Of My Mind showcases transition toward mature songwriting and production styles.
- Stevie Wonder experiments with various instruments demonstrating remarkable multi-instrumental talent.
- Album represents pivotal moment marking artistic growth and personal expression freedom.
- Funky rhythms blend seamlessly with soulful melodies creating distinctive musical atmosphere.
- Themes of love and introspection recur frequently in Wonder's thoughtful lyrics.
- Innovative studio techniques highlight Wonder's forward-thinking approach to recording music.
- Self-contained production reflects growing confidence in his musical abilities overall.
- Album bridges earlier hits with more experimental sounds yet to come later.
- Emotional depth resonates strongly through heartfelt vocal deliveries and arrangements.
- Wonder's keyboard skills shine brightly adding rich harmonic layers everywhere.
- Release date signifies important milestone in his long illustrious career journey.
- Music serves as direct reflection of the artist's mind and imagination.

INSIGHTS
- Creative freedom fosters deeper artistic authenticity and personal musical evolution.
- Technology integration enhances expressive possibilities within traditional soul music frameworks.
- Self-production empowers artists to realize unique visions without external constraints.
- Introspection fuels meaningful songwriting that connects universally with diverse listeners.
- Early experimentation lays groundwork for groundbreaking later achievements and innovations.

HABITS

- He collaborates closely with engineers to achieve desired sonic qualities precisely.
- Regular listening to diverse music inspires continuous creative development and growth.

FACTS
- Music Of My Mind released on March 3 in the year 1972.
- Stevie Wonder served as primary producer for this particular album project.
- Album features extensive use of synthesizers and keyboard instruments throughout.
- It marks Wonder's first self-produced effort under Motown record label.
- The record contains nine original songs written solely by Stevie Wonder.
