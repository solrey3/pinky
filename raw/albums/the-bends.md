# Radiohead — The Bends

**Release Date:** 1995-03-13
**Label:** Parlophone
**Producer:** John Leckie, Radiohead

## Overview

The Bends is the second studio album by the English rock band Radiohead, released on 13 March 1995 by Parlophone. It was produced by John Leckie and Radiohead. Most of the album was recorded at RAK Studios in London between August and November 1994. The album marked a shift from the grunge influences of their debut album *Pablo Honey* (1993) toward more expansive guitar rock and introspective lyrics.

The Bends was Radiohead's first UK top-five album, reaching number four on the UK Albums Chart. It reached number 88 on the US Billboard 200. It produced five singles: "My Iron Lung", "High and Dry", "Fake Plastic Trees", "Just", and "Street Spirit (Fade Out)". The album received critical acclaim, and is frequently named among the best albums of all time. In 2020, Rolling Stone ranked it number 276 on its list of the "500 Greatest Albums of All Time".
