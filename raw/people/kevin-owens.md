# Kevin Owens

**Real Name:** Kevin Steen
**Born:** 1984, Marieville QC
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2000–present
**Personal Rank:** #52 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#52. The most versatile performer in WWE today. Owens can work as a legitimate heel, a comedic face, and a serious babyface — sometimes within the same feud. His debut in WWE (NXT, 2015) immediately established his credibility by defeating John Cena in his first main roster match. The Prize Fighter character operates on a simple but effective premise: he does things for money.

## Career Highlights

- WWE Universal Champion; IC Champion; US Champion; NXT Champion
- His match with John Cena at Elimination Chamber 2015 — his first main roster match — ending in victory established him immediately
- His Universal Championship win (2016) was one of WWE's best feel-good moments of that year
- His comedic segments with Sami Zayn (Honorary Uce; KO Show) are the funniest in WWE in a decade
- The Stunner finish ties him explicitly to Steve Austin's legacy while acknowledging his own WWE generation

## Why They Matter

Owens and Zayn's friendship is professional wrestling's best genuine friendship angle — partly because their real-life friendship is real. Their tag team work and feud-then-reconciliation arc is the best WWE storytelling since the Bloodline saga.
