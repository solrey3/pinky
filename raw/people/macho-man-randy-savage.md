# Macho Man Randy Savage

**Real Name:** Randy Poffo
**Born:** 1952, Columbus OH
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1973–2004
**Personal Rank:** #8 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#8 all-time. One of the most complete performers in wrestling history — elite promo ability, genuine ring psychology, and a larger-than-life character that was simultaneously absurd and compelling. His partnership with Miss Elizabeth is wrestling's most iconic romantic story. The WrestleMania III match with Ricky Steamboat is the bout that proved WWE could do world-class technical wrestling.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple WWF/WCW Champion; first King of the Ring
- WM3 vs. Steamboat is on every list of the greatest matches ever; both men were extraordinary
- The Cream of the Crop promo ('Ooooh yeah!') is the most imitated promo in wrestling
- Elizabeth's involvement in his storylines gave his character genuine emotional stakes
- Slim Jim commercials made him a mainstream pop culture figure in the early 90s

## Why They Matter

Savage died in 2011 from a heart attack while driving — a loss that deprived wrestling of a living legend who had never received the full-length Hall of Fame recognition his career demanded. His induction came posthumously in 2015. OOooh YEAH.
