# Klay Thompson

**Born:** 1990, Los Angeles CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 2011–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

Splash Bro. Four championships with Golden State, two-year recovery from Achilles and knee injuries (2019–2021). His 37-point quarter (October 2015) against Sacramento is the most concentrated single-period scoring effort in NBA history. His catch-and-shoot three-pointer release time is the fastest of any NBA player.

## Career Highlights

- 4x NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022 Golden State Warriors)
- 37 points in the third quarter against Sacramento (2015) — the most in a single quarter in NBA history
- His Achilles and knee injury recovery (2019–2021, two consecutive seasons missed) and return to championship basketball is the greatest multi-injury comeback in NBA history
- His off-the-ball movement and catch-and-shoot mechanics are the most advanced in the league
- His offensive arsenal — off screens, in transition, catch-and-shoot — is entirely distinct from Curry's, making them the most complementary shooting pair in history
