# Bobby Lashley

**Real Name:** Bobby Lashley
**Born:** 1976, Colorado Springs CO
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / MMA fighter
**Active:** 2005–present
**Personal Rank:** #72 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#72. The most legitimately dangerous-seeming wrestler on the WWE roster. Lashley's Division I wrestling background and legitimate MMA career (15-2) give his moveset a credibility that scripted wrestlers cannot manufacture. His WWE Championship run (2021) was his best character work (Bobby Lashley's All Mighty era with MVP is the best undercard faction work of that year).

## Career Highlights

- WWE Champion; ECW Champion; ROH Tag Champion
- His All Mighty WWE Championship run (2021) with MVP is the best heel authority-backed character work of that year
- His legitimate MMA record (15-2) is comparable to Lesnar's as a legitimacy credential
- The Hurt Lock (full nelson) is one of wrestling's most convincing submissions — hard to escape from physically
- His feud with Bill Goldberg (2023) was unnecessary but his match quality throughout the decade is consistent

## Why They Matter

Lashley is the most athletically complete performer on WWE's roster — a fact that has been acknowledged and underutilized in roughly equal measure.
