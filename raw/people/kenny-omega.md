# Kenny Omega

**Real Name:** Tyson Smith
**Born:** 1983, Winnipeg MB
**Nationality:** Canadian
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1999–present
**Personal Rank:** #23 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#23. The most important figure in international wrestling of the last decade. His NJPW run (2015–2019) produced the greatest match catalog of any single wrestler in the modern era — Okada I-IV, Tanahashi, Naito, Osprey. As AEW co-founder, he built the company's stylistic identity. His 'The Best Bout Machine' nickname is borne out by his match record.

## Career Highlights

- IWGP Heavyweight Champion; AEW World Champion; AEW All-Atlantic Champion
- The Okada IV match at NJPW Dominion 2018 (7 stars, Meltzer) is considered the greatest match ever by many metrics
- His Bullet Club run (2014–2018) made NJPW internationally commercially viable
- AEW co-founder; his creative vision shaped AEW's early identity
- His matches in Canada vs. young Konosuke Takeshita in 2017 predated the talent development that made AEW

## Why They Matter

Omega's match catalog is an argument for wrestling as a serious athletic and narrative art form. The Okada series is four chapters of a story told entirely in 60 minutes of ring work. No other wrestler has produced that quality over that duration.
