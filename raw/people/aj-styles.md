# AJ Styles

**Real Name:** Allen Jones
**Born:** 1977, Jacksonville NC
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1998–present
**Personal Rank:** #17 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#17. The best wrestler in the world for a sustained period (2003–2010 TNA/NJPW) before WWE even signed him. His NJPW run with the Bullet Club established the concept of independent wrestling's world-class status. His WWE debut at Royal Rumble 2016 was one of the greatest star-arrival moments of the modern era. The Phenomenal One nickname is not hyperbole.

## Career Highlights

- TNA World Champion; WWE Champion; IWGP Champion
- His match with Samoa Joe at NJPW in 2009 is one of the best matches of that decade
- Bullet Club founder and the brand that made NJPW a viable international wrestling entity
- WWE debut at Royal Rumble 2016 — the pop when his music hit was one of the loudest in years
- His matches with John Cena, Roman Reigns, and CM Punk in WWE are consistently the best on the card

## Why They Matter

AJ Styles built his legacy in promotions that most of his eventual WWE audience had never watched — and then arrived in WWE as a finished product. That backstory is part of what makes the Phenomenal One genuine rather than corporate-manufactured.
