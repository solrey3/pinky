# Ray Allen

**Born:** 1975, Merced CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 1996–2014
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The greatest shooter in NBA history before Curry redefined the category. All-time leader in three-pointers made at retirement (2,973). Two championships (2008 Celtics, 2013 Heat). His corner three in Game 6 of the 2013 Finals (sending it to overtime, saving the Heat from elimination) is the most important single shot in NBA Finals history.

## Career Highlights

- 2x NBA Champion (2008 Boston Celtics, 2013 Miami Heat)
- All-time three-point leader at retirement (2,973 — later passed by Curry)
- His 2013 Finals Game 6 corner three is the most important shot in modern Finals history
- His shooting mechanics are studied as the gold standard for off-ball guard shooting technique
- 10x All-Star; 1x Sportsmanship Award — he is the NBA's most respected person
