# Nikola Jokić

**Born:** 1995, Sombor, Serbia
**Nationality:** Serbian
**Position:** C
**Active:** 2015–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 5 (#23)

## Summary

The most efficient offensive player in NBA history and the first genuine passing center at the MVP level. Three regular-season MVPs (2021, 2022, 2024) and one championship (2023 Denver Nuggets). Jokić's combination of vision, touch, and decision-making is genuinely unprecedented — he averages triple-doubles at a per-game rate that no center has approached.

## Career Highlights

- 3x NBA MVP (2021, 2022, 2024); 1x NBA Champion (2023); 1x Finals MVP
- His 2021–22 season (26.4 ppg, 13.8 rpg, 7.9 apg) is the most statistically complete season for a center in NBA history
- All-time leader in triple-doubles for centers
- The first center to win the MVP award three times since Kareem
- His 2023 NBA Finals performance (30.2 ppg, 14.0 rpg, 7.8 apg) is the most complete Finals performance in history by a big man

## Why They Matter

Jokić is the best basketball player in the world right now and the most interesting statistical outlier in NBA history — a center who leads his team in assists most nights. His career is still being written, but three MVPs before 30 puts him in the conversation for the greatest center run since Kareem.
