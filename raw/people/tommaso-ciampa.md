# Blackheart

**Real Name:** Tommaso Ciampa
**Born:** 1985, Boston MA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2006–present
**Personal Rank:** #41 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#41. The best pure heel in NXT history. His turn on Johnny Gargano (2017) launched one of the most emotionally invested feuds in wrestling history. The Blackheart character — methodical, sadistic, operating under a coherent internal logic — is the most psychologically complete villain in modern developmental wrestling. His promo ability elevated NXT's storytelling ambitions.

## Career Highlights

- NXT Champion (multiple reigns)
- His unsanctioned match with Gargano at TakeOver New Orleans (2018) is one of the greatest matches in NXT history — from the villain's perspective
- His heel turn on DIY at NXT Chicago (2017) is the best single turn in NXT history
- His DIY reunion with Gargano is one of wrestling's more believable 'we need each other' stories
- His main roster run (as Mamma Mia's Ciampa) showed comedic range that his heel work obscured

## Why They Matter

Ciampa's Blackheart represents NXT wrestling storytelling at its ceiling — a character whose motivations were internally coherent and whose matches supported the narrative. His career is a tutorial in how to work a feud.
