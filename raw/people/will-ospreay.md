# Will Ospreay

**Real Name:** William Ospreay
**Born:** 1993, Havant, England
**Nationality:** British
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2012–present
**Personal Rank:** #32 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#32. The best aerial/technical hybrid wrestler in the world right now. His NJPW run (2016–2023) produced some of the highest-rated matches in history. His AEW debut and championship run have established him as a legitimate main event draw for the first time outside Japan. His matches with Kenny Omega, Kazuchika Okada, and Zack Sabre Jr. are repeatedly among the year's best.

## Career Highlights

- IWGP World Heavyweight Champion; AEW International/Continental Champion
- His Okada match at NJPW WrestleKingdom 14 (★★★★¾) is the best match of 2020
- His matches with Zack Sabre Jr. are the best technical exchanges in the world today
- The Hidden Blade (stiff elbow) is a credible finishing strike in a cruiserweight-sized package
- His AEW debut pop was the largest non-WWE debut reaction in years

## Why They Matter

Ospreay is the successor to Kenny Omega's position as the best wrestler in Japan — but he has also successfully transitioned to AEW in a way that suggests he can be a top star anywhere in the world.
