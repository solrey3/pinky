# The Heartbreak Kid / HBK

**Real Name:** Shawn Michaels
**Born:** 1965, Chandler AZ
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1984–2010
**Personal Rank:** #3 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#3 all-time. Pound-for-pound the best in-ring performer in WWE history. The Michaels/Undertaker WrestleMania matches (25 and 26) are universally considered the greatest in WrestleMania history. His comeback from a debilitating back injury (1998–2002) and four-year retirement is one of wrestling's great comeback stories. DX with Triple H defined mid-Attitude Era comedy.

## Career Highlights

- Inaugural Intercontinental/WWF Champion; multiple reigns
- WrestleMania 25 vs. Undertaker rated ★★★★¾ by virtually everyone — often called the greatest WM match
- His Royal Rumble 1995 performance (entered at #1, lasted over 38 minutes) is a physical marvel
- Sweet Chin Music is wrestling's most elegant superkick finisher
- Three-time WWE Champion; won the first Royal Rumble match in 1995

## Why They Matter

The Showstopper, Mr. WrestleMania. HBK's ability to perform at the highest level on the biggest stages defines his legacy. He is the reason the term 'five-star match' became currency in wrestling discourse.
