# Kareem Abdul-Jabbar

**Born:** 1947, Manhattan NY
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** C
**Active:** 1969–1989
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 2 (#3)

## Summary

The NBA's all-time scoring leader (until LeBron passed him in 2023). Kareem's skyhook is the most unstoppable shot in basketball history — a hook shot with such extension that no defender ever developed an effective counter. Six championships (one with Milwaukee, five with the Lakers), six MVP awards, nineteen All-Star selections.

## Career Highlights

- All-time NBA scoring leader (until 2023): 38,387 points
- 6x NBA MVP — the most in NBA history
- The skyhook: uncontested as the most unstoppable fundamental shot in basketball history
- 6x NBA Champion; performed at an elite level from 1969 to 1989 — 20 consecutive seasons
- His UCLA career (3x NCAA Champion, never lost a game in three seasons) is the most dominant college basketball career in history

## Why They Matter

Kareem played in an era when his dominance wasn't televised nationally until late in his career, which has affected his popular legacy. His statistical record speaks for itself. The skyhook is the most technically perfect offensive move in basketball — designed specifically to be unblockable — and he perfected it.
