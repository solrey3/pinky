# Tracy McGrady

**Born:** 1979, Bartow FL
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF/SG
**Active:** 1997–2013
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The most gifted offensive player who never won a playoff series. T-Mac scored 13 points in 35 seconds against San Antonio (2004) — the most dramatic fourth-quarter comeback in regular season history. Two scoring titles, one MVP finish as runner-up. His career was limited by knee and shoulder injuries from 25 onward.

## Career Highlights

- 2x scoring champion (2002–03); 7x All-Star
- His 13 points in 35 seconds against San Antonio (2004) is the most dramatic late-game comeback in NBA regular season history
- He and Vince Carter (his cousin) were the most athletically gifted wing pairing in the same era
- His partnership with Yao Ming in Houston was the most culturally significant international pairing in NBA history
- His early retirement (effectively 30 due to injuries) deprived the NBA of his peak years
