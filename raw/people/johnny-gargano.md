# Johnny Wrestling

**Real Name:** Johnny Gargano
**Born:** 1987, Cleveland OH
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2005–present
**Personal Rank:** #38 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#38. The greatest NXT performer in WWE history. Gargano's trilogy with Tommaso Ciampa (2017–2019) is the best sustained feud in NXT history and one of the best in wrestling. The unsanctioned match at NXT TakeOver: New Orleans (2018) is the best singles match in NXT history. His DIY tag team with Ciampa was the best tag team in WWE across their run.

## Career Highlights

- NXT Champion; NXT Tag Champion (DIY with Ciampa)
- The unsanctioned match with Ciampa at TakeOver New Orleans (2018) is ★★★★¾ and the best NXT match ever
- His three-match series with Adam Cole at NXT TakeOver events produced three match-of-the-year candidates in one year
- DIY vs. The Authors of Pain (War Games 2017) is the best War Games match in the modern era
- His main roster return (2022) in Chicago (CM Punk-level pop) validated his connection with wrestling's core audience

## Why They Matter

Gargano's career is the NXT system working at its highest level. He built a fanbase through pure excellence in a developmental brand and emerged as a main event-caliber performer on sheer craft.
