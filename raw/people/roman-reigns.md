# Roman Reigns

**Real Name:** Leati Joseph Anoaʻi
**Born:** 1985, Pensacola FL
**Nationality:** Samoan-American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2010–present
**Personal Rank:** #10 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#10 all-time. The greatest 'turn' in wrestling history: a years-long failed babyface push (2014–2020) that the crowd rejected violently, transformed into the most complete heel character in wrestling when he aligned with Paul Heyman as the 'Tribal Chief' in 2020. His Undisputed WWE Universal Championship reign (2020–2024) is the longest world title reign in the modern era at 1,316 days.

## Career Highlights

- Longest Universal/WWE Undisputed title reign in modern history (1,316 days)
- The Tribal Chief character and Bloodline storyline is the best long-term wrestling storyline of the 2020s
- His transformation from rejected babyface to beloved heel is the most complete character redemption in wrestling
- His feuds with Jey Uso, Sami Zayn, and Cody Rhodes elevated everyone around him
- Roman vs. Cody Rhodes at WM39 and WM40 were the biggest WrestleMania main events in 20 years

## Why They Matter

The Bloodline story is the best wrestling has produced since the Attitude Era. Reigns went from a man the crowd refused to cheer to a character so compelling that his eventual loss to Cody Rhodes felt genuinely earned. His Samoan family dynasty adds a real-world dimension to the fiction.
