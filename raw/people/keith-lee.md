# Keith Lee

**Real Name:** Keith Lee
**Born:** 1985, Wichita Falls TX
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2009–present
**Personal Rank:** #76 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#76. The most physically unique performer of his generation — a 320-pound man who can execute hurricanranas, springboards, and moonsaults with athleticism that defies his size. His NXT Double Champion run (2020) — holding the NXT and NXT NA Championships simultaneously — briefly made him the most exciting performer in wrestling. His AEW debut has been hampered by injury.

## Career Highlights

- NXT Champion; NXT NA Champion (held simultaneously)
- Holding both NXT titles simultaneously is one of the most physically impressive achievement combinations in developmental wrestling
- The Pounce and Grizzly Magnum combination finishes are the most size-appropriate moves in wrestling
- His NXT work with Karrion Kross and Dominik Mysterio showed his range beyond pure power offense
- His Boundless character — a phrase that applies literally to his movement range

## Why They Matter

Lee's ceiling is genuinely limitless — if he has a healthy run in any major promotion, the results will be extraordinary. His career has been defined by injuries arriving exactly when his momentum peaks.
