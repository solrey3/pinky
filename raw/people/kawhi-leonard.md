# Kawhi Leonard

**Born:** 1991, Los Angeles CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF/SG
**Active:** 2011–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 5 (#24)

## Summary

The most efficient two-way wing in NBA history. Two championships (2014 Spurs, 2019 Raptors), two Finals MVPs, two Defensive Player of the Year awards. Kawhi's 'Fun Guy' and 'Board Man Gets Paid' cultural moments, combined with his mechanical shooting form and anaconda-length arms, make him uniquely compelling. His 2019 buzzer-beater against Philadelphia is the single most dramatic shot in modern NBA history.

## Career Highlights

- 2x NBA Champion (2014 Spurs, 2019 Raptors); 2x Finals MVP; 2x Defensive Player of the Year
- His 2019 series against Philadelphia (The Shot, Game 7 buzzer-beater) is the most dramatic single playoff sequence of the 2010s
- His career shooting efficiency from both inside and outside is the highest for a wing player in NBA history
- His 2014 Finals performance (17.8 ppg on 61.4% shooting) effectively neutralized LeBron James
- His post-injury (2018–2019) playoff run — 60 games played, two championship rounds, one title — is the most efficient championship arc in modern NBA history

## Why They Matter

Leonard's value proposition is simple: he makes every shot he should take, guards every player he's asked to guard, and doesn't need ball movement to produce. His only weakness as a player is that he is a free agent every three years. His only weakness as a cultural figure is that he appears to have no identifiable personality, which makes everything he does say funnier.
