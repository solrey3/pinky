# Nature Boy

**Real Name:** Ric Flair
**Born:** 1949, Memphis TN
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1972–2011 (various returns)
**Personal Rank:** #2 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#2 all-time. The greatest professional wrestler in terms of pure in-ring work and character longevity across multiple decades and promotions. Sixteen-time world champion (NWA, WCW, WWF) whose feud portfolio — Steamboat, Sting, Dusty, Harley Race, Randy Savage — covers the entire history of the sport. WOOOOO is the most recognizable sound in wrestling.

## Career Highlights

- 16-time world champion across NWA/WCW/WWF
- His trilogy with Ricky Steamboat (1989) is the gold standard of technical wrestling
- Built the Four Horsemen into wrestling's most influential faction
- Trained and mentored an entire generation including Batista, Triple H, and Randy Orton
- His WrestleMania 24 retirement match with Shawn Michaels (2008) is the best retirement story in wrestling

## Why They Matter

The Rolex-wearing, private-jet-flying, limousine-riding character was simultaneously aspirational and ridiculous — which made it perfect. Flair's ability to work either as a hero or villain and deliver a great match against anyone, anywhere, is unparalleled. His longevity (four decades of relevance) is the most remarkable career in wrestling history.
