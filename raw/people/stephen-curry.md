# Stephen Curry

**Born:** 1988, Charlotte NC
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 2009–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 2 (#6)

## Summary

The greatest shooter in basketball history and the player who fundamentally changed how the game is played. Curry's ability to shoot accurately from 30+ feet made the three-point line a primary offensive weapon system rather than a supplemental one. Four championships, two regular-season MVPs (one unanimous), two scoring titles. The 2015–16 Warriors season (73-9) was built around his unprecedented shooting range.

## Career Highlights

- 4x NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)
- Two regular-season MVPs, including unanimous 2016 (first in history)
- All-time leader in three-pointers made (3,747 and counting)
- His 2015–16 season (30.1 ppg, 45.4% from three on record volume) is the most statistically revolutionary season in NBA history
- His shooting fundamentals — quick release, deep range, off-the-dribble accuracy — are studied by every level of basketball coach

## Why They Matter

Curry didn't just break shooting records — he changed where basketball teams shoot from. The corner three and the deep logo three are now standard offensive weapons because Curry proved they were viable. His influence on basketball at every level is greater than any player since Jordan.
