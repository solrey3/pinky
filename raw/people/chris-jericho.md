# Chris Jericho

**Real Name:** Christopher Irvine
**Born:** 1970, Manhasset NY
**Nationality:** Canadian-American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1990–present
**Personal Rank:** #11 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#11 all-time. The most adaptable star in wrestling history across WWE, AEW, and multiple character reinventions. The first Undisputed WWE Champion (holding both WWF and WCW titles simultaneously). His Le Champion/Inner Circle/Jericho Appreciation Society/Learning Tree AEW characters show an ability to reinvent that most wrestlers cannot sustain past 40. Also a legitimate rock musician (Fozzy).

## Career Highlights

- First Undisputed WWE Champion (2001)
- Y2J debut on Raw (1999, interrupting The Rock) is one of the best debuts in wrestling history
- AEW founding star and the most important signing in AEW's early credibility
- His matches with Dean Malenko, Shawn Michaels, CM Punk, and Kenny Omega span three decades of quality
- Fozzy has toured globally — he lives the rock star lifestyle because he actually is one

## Why They Matter

Jericho at 54 is still delivering quality matches in AEW, which is either inspiring or unbelievable depending on your threshold. His ability to reinvent himself — from Lionheart to Y2J to heel Jericho to Le Champion to Jericho Appreciation Society — is unique in wrestling.
