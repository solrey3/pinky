# Kyrie Irving

**Born:** 1992, Melbourne, Australia
**Nationality:** Australian-American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 2011–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The most skilled ball-handler in NBA history. One championship (2016 Cleveland Cavaliers), one NBA Finals-winning shot (Game 7, 2016). Kyrie's left-hand layup off the backboard in the final minute of Game 7 is the most important individual shot in NBA history. His personal conduct (flat-earth claims, antisemitism controversies) has complicated a basketball legacy that remains elite.

## Career Highlights

- 1x NBA Champion (2016 Cleveland Cavaliers); 1x All-Star Game MVP
- His Game 7 three-pointer over Curry (2016 Finals) is the most important shot in NBA history
- 7x All-Star; his handle (Euro-step, behind-the-back, hesitation) is technically the best in basketball history
- His 2012 Olympics performance and All-Star Game performance demonstrate skills beyond his regular-season stats
- His personal controversies have defined his public legacy more than his basketball should allow
