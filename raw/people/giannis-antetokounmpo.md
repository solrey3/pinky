# Giannis Antetokounmpo

**Born:** 1994, Athens, Greece
**Nationality:** Greek-Nigerian
**Position:** PF/C
**Active:** 2013–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Tier 5 (#25)

## Summary

The Greek Freak. The most physically unique player in the modern NBA — a 6'11" player with point guard ball-handling skills and center-level finishing at the rim. Two regular-season MVPs (2019, 2020), one championship (2021 Milwaukee Bucks), one Finals MVP. His 2021 Finals performance (35.2 ppg, 13.2 rpg, 5.0 apg) against the Suns is the most dominant individual Finals performance since Shaq in 2001.

## Career Highlights

- 2x NBA MVP (2019, 2020); 1x NBA Champion (2021); 1x Finals MVP; 1x DPOY
- His 2021 Finals performance (35.2 ppg on 61.8% shooting) is the most physically dominant Finals since Shaq 2001
- His family's immigration story (from Nigeria through Greece) and his rise from poverty to NBA success is the most compelling modern NBA backstory
- His Euro-step at full speed is the most unstoppable single move in the modern game
- His decision to stay in Milwaukee (2020 supermax extension) is the most franchise-loyal decision by an MVP-caliber player since Tim Duncan

## Why They Matter

Giannis represents the global NBA — a Greek-Nigerian player who grew up in Athens without basketball training before becoming the game's most physically dominant force. His loyalty to Milwaukee (rejecting larger markets) is also an argument about what commitment to a franchise and a city looks like.
