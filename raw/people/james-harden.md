# James Harden

**Born:** 1989, Los Angeles CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 2009–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The greatest volume scorer in the modern NBA and the player who most completely exploited the free throw structure of the modern game. One MVP (2018), one scoring champion (six consecutive titles), never won a championship. His step-back three-pointer is the most imitated individual offensive move of the 2010s.

## Career Highlights

- 1x NBA MVP (2018); 6x scoring champion
- His step-back three-pointer is the most imitated individual offensive move in the modern game
- His 2018–19 Houston Rockets season (36.1 ppg on 44.2% shooting) is the greatest pure scoring season of the 3-point era
- His 60-point performance in three quarters (December 2019) is one of the most efficient short-duration scoring efforts in history
- The RAPTOR analytics show him as one of the 10 most valuable players in NBA history
