# Clyde Drexler

**Born:** 1962, New Orleans LA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 1983–1998
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The Glide. One championship (1995 Houston Rockets, with Hakeem), Finals runner-up (1992 with Portland). Drexler's comparison with Jordan — the Blazers drafting him over Jordan in 1984 is one of basketball's most painful front-office decisions retrospectively — defines his era. His 1992 Finals against Jordan was the most stylistically comparable guard matchup in Finals history.

## Career Highlights

- 1x NBA Champion (1995 Houston Rockets); 10x All-Star; 1x Slam Dunk Contest winner
- His 1992 Finals against Jordan (the Shrug game) is the definitive Jordan-era great-player matchup
- The most complete wing of his era outside of Jordan — scoring, rebounding, and defense
- Portland's 1992 championship team was potentially as good as Chicago's 1992 team — the wrong team won that series
- His reunion with Hakeem (1995) is the best veteran-acquisition storyline in NBA history
