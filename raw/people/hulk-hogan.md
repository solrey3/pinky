# Hulk Hogan

**Real Name:** Terry Bollea
**Born:** 1953, Augusta GA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1977–2012
**Personal Rank:** #50 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#50. The man who made wrestling mainstream. Hogan's 1980s run with Vince McMahon transformed wrestling from a regional attraction to a national entertainment product. WrestleMania, Saturday Night's Main Event, Rock 'n' Wrestling Connection — all built on the Hulkamaniacs. His in-ring ability was limited; his drawing power was limitless. His nWo Hollywood heel turn is one of the greatest twists in wrestling history.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple WWF/WCW World Champion
- His 1980s WWF run is the most commercially successful period in wrestling history
- The nWo Hollywood turn at Bash at the Beach 1996 is wrestling's most surprising pivot
- His match with Andre at WM3 (93,173 at the Silverdome) remains the largest reported wrestling attendance in history
- His racism scandal (2015) and subsequent WWE/industry treatment reflects how complicated his legacy is

## Why They Matter

Hogan's placement at #50 below wrestlers who were better in the ring but less famous reflects a specific values system. His cultural importance cannot be overstated; his in-ring ceiling was real. The racist audio tapes complicate any clean legacy statement.
