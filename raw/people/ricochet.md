# Ricochet

**Real Name:** Trevor Mann
**Born:** 1988, Paducah KY
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2007–present
**Personal Rank:** #74 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#74. The most technically perfect aerial wrestler in American wrestling today. Ricochet's flips — the 630 splash, the Fosbury flop, the shooting star press — are executed with a precision and height that makes them look performed rather than risked. His NXT North American Championship run (2019) produced some of the best short-reign championship matches in NXT history.

## Career Highlights

- NXT NA Champion; WWE IC Champion
- The '630 splash' (630-degree senton) is the most technically demanding finishing move performed on a regular basis
- His match with Adam Cole at NXT TakeOver New York (2019) is the best NXT NA Championship match in the title's history
- His PWG work (2014–2018) built a reputation that his NXT career confirmed
- His inability to break through WWE's main event ceiling despite obvious ability is a recurring talent management frustration

## Why They Matter

Ricochet is what the Attitude Era would have called 'a spot monkey' — and that dismissal misses the point. His aerial work is executed with a precision that requires genuine athletic discipline, not just daring.
