# Damian Lillard

**Born:** 1990, Oakland CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 2012–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

Dame Time. The greatest late-game scorer in the modern NBA who does not have a championship. Seven All-Star selections, multiple All-NBA. His 37-foot buzzer-beater against OKC (2019 playoffs) is the most spectacular buzzer-beater since Kawhi's in 2019. His transfer to Milwaukee (2023) was the most-discussed player movement of that year.

## Career Highlights

- 7x All-Star; multiple All-NBA First Team
- His 37-foot buzzer-beater against Russell Westbrook/OKC (2019 playoffs) is the most spectacular game-winner since Jordan's in 1989
- Dame Time is the most reliable clutch fourth-quarter scoring reputation in the modern NBA
- His Portland career (2012–2023) was the longest single-franchise superstar tenure of his era
- His 2024 Finals appearance with Milwaukee is his closest championship approach
