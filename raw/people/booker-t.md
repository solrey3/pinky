# Booker T

**Real Name:** Booker Huffman
**Born:** 1965, Houston TX
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1989–2012
**Personal Rank:** #30 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#30. One of the most criminally underutilized main event talents in wrestling history. A five-time WCW champion and multiple-time WWE champion whose ring work was consistently excellent and character work — from Harlem Heat to King Booker — showed real range. Tell me he didn't just say that! SPINAROONIE. His commentary career has been as long as his wrestling one.

## Career Highlights

- 5x WCW Champion; multiple WWE Heavyweight and IC titles; King Booker
- His WCW tag team career with brother Stevie Ray (Harlem Heat) was the best black tag team in wrestling history
- King Booker character (2006–2007) was his most complete character and his most underrated run
- His triple threat matches in WWE (2003–2004) were consistently among the better matches on WWE PPVs
- His NXT Dusty Rhodes Classic tournament work extended his credibility into the 2010s

## Why They Matter

Booker T's longevity was built on genuine athletic gifts and a willingness to do whatever character work the company needed. His 'Tell me he didn't just say that!' commentary moments with Jim Ross are among the best in wrestling history.
