# Stone Cold Steve Austin

**Real Name:** Steve Austin
**Born:** 1964, Victoria TX
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1989–2003, 2011, 2022
**Personal Rank:** #1 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#1 all-time personal ranking. The defining star of the WWE Attitude Era (1997–2001). Three-time WWF/WWE Champion whose anti-authority 'Stone Cold' persona — brawling, beer-drinking, middle-finger raising — redefined babyface wrestling for the post-kayfabe age. His feud with Vince McMahon is the most profitable storyline in wrestling history and invented the 'boss vs. employee' template that has been copied ever since.

## Career Highlights

- Attitude Era's defining star; 6x WWE/WWF Champion
- The Austin 3:16 promo at King of the Ring 1996 launched his main event career instantly
- Stone Cold Stunner became wrestling's most recognized finisher
- His feuds with McMahon drove WWE to crush WCW in the Monday Night Wars
- Retired young (39) due to neck and knee damage from a relentless physical style

## Why They Matter

The peak of American professional wrestling commercial popularity. Austin's everyman working-class rage resonated in a way that no scripted character had before him. The Attitude Era's success was built almost entirely on his shoulders — and it revived a company that had nearly been bankrupted by the Monday Night Wars.
