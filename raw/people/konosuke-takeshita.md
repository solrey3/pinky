# Konosuke Takeshita

**Real Name:** Konosuke Takeshita
**Born:** 1996, Saitama, Japan
**Nationality:** Japanese
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2014–present
**Personal Rank:** #53 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#53. The most exciting young talent in professional wrestling. His DDT Pro career built his reputation in Japan before his AEW debut turned him into an international star. His matches with Jon Moxley (multiple), Bryan Danielson, and Kenny Omega have produced some of AEW's best in-ring work. The Blue Thunder Bomb is the most impactful power move from a 220-pound wrestler working today.

## Career Highlights

- DDT Pro champion; AEW International/Continental champion
- His match with Moxley at AEW All Out 2022 is the best American television match of that year
- His work with Don Callis Family in AEW gave him a character arc beyond pure wrestling excellence
- The Blue Thunder Bomb delivered with Takeshita's conviction is genuinely electrifying
- His trajectory from DDT opener to AEW main event suggests a ceiling as high as Omega's by his mid-30s

## Why They Matter

Takeshita is wrestling's most exciting developmental story — a talent so good that AEW's most discerning audience responded immediately, before WWE's mainstream audience knew who he was.
