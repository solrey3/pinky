# Luka Dončić

**Born:** 2000, Ljubljana, Slovenia
**Nationality:** Slovenian
**Position:** SG/PG
**Active:** 2018–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The most statistically complete young player in NBA history. Dončić's career averages (27.7 ppg, 8.6 rpg, 8.2 apg as of 2026) are unprecedented for his position — he's averaging more assists per game than most PGs while playing off-ball. His 2024 Dallas Mavericks Finals run (29.2 ppg in the series) is the best young player Finals performance in a generation.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple All-NBA First Team; 2024 NBA Finals appearance
- His career per-game averages (27.7/8.6/8.2) through 25 are the most complete for a wing in NBA history
- His step-back three-pointer (inherited from Harden, refined to perfection) is the most unstoppable shot in the modern game
- His EuroLeague career (MVP at 19 with Real Madrid) is the most decorated pre-NBA career in history
- The Most Improved Player and Rookie of the Year debate around his 2019 performance reflects how good he was immediately
