# Emanuel Ginóbili

**Born:** 1977, Bahía Blanca, Argentina
**Nationality:** Argentine
**Position:** SG
**Active:** 2002–2018
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

Manu. Four championships (2003, 2005, 2007, 2014 San Antonio Spurs). The greatest sixth man in NBA history and the primary Argentine basketball icon. His Euro-step is the most imitated basketball move in the world — every NBA player does a Euro-step now because Manu popularized it. His bat catch (caught a bat mid-game) is the bravest moment in NBA regular season history.

## Career Highlights

- 4x NBA Champion (2003, 2005, 2007, 2014 Spurs); 1x All-Star
- The Euro-step is now universal in basketball because Manu popularized it at the NBA level
- 2004 Olympic gold medal with Argentina — one of the greatest upsets in Olympic basketball history (beat the USA)
- His longevity (16 seasons at high level as a 6th man or starter) is the Spurs system in one career
- His decision to remain in San Antonio (rejecting larger markets) is the most complete expression of Spurs team-first culture
