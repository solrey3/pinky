# Tony Parker

**Born:** 1982, Bruges, Belgium
**Nationality:** French
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 2001–2019
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The most effective French player in NBA history and the point guard of the Spurs dynasty. Four championships, one Finals MVP (2007). Parker's floater over shot-blockers and his pick-and-roll operation with Duncan and Ginobili is the most successful three-man offensive unit in the Spurs dynasty.

## Career Highlights

- 4x NBA Champion (2003, 2005, 2007, 2014 Spurs); 1x Finals MVP
- 6x All-Star; his career with the Spurs is the model for point guard integration into a system
- His 2007 Finals performance (24.5 ppg on 57.1% shooting) against LeBron's Cavaliers is his best playoff series
- His floater over Dwight Howard in the 2012 Western Conference Finals is the single best shot over the best shot-blocker in that era
- His post-NBA career in France and his Spurs legacy represent the most complete French sports career in NBA history
