# The Rock

**Real Name:** Dwayne Johnson
**Born:** 1972, Hayward CA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / actor
**Active:** 1996–2004, 2011–2013, 2022 (wrestling); film career ongoing
**Personal Rank:** #5 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#5 all-time. The most charismatic performer in wrestling history and the only wrestler to become a genuine A-list Hollywood star. His 2001–2004 run defined the transition from Attitude Era to Ruthless Aggression. The Rock's promo ability — the catchphrases, the raised eyebrow, the People's Elbow — was operating at a level of entertainment no one else touched.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple WWF/WWE Champion; Royal Rumble 2000 winner
- Rock vs. Austin trilogy at WrestleMania is the most star-powered match series in wrestling history
- Jannetty'd his tag partner Mick Foley into a singles main event run and both came out elevated
- Returned in 2011 to feud with John Cena for two WrestleManias
- Became the highest-paid actor in Hollywood — Jumanji, Fast & Furious, and franchise anchoring

## Why They Matter

The Rock's crossover success validated wrestling as a legitimate talent pipeline to mainstream entertainment. His charisma was so large it was always going to outgrow the squared circle. His return feuds with Cena showed he could still do it at 40. The Roman Reigns 'acknowledgment' storyline in 2024 was his best work since 2003.
