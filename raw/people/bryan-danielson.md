# American Dragon / Daniel Bryan

**Real Name:** Bryan Danielson
**Born:** 1981, Aberdeen WA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1999–present
**Personal Rank:** #7 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#7 all-time. The greatest pure technical wrestler of the modern era and the most unlikely mainstream star in wrestling history. A small (5'10") indie wrestling prodigy who became a WWE mega-star through organic fan support — the 'YES! Movement' in 2013–2014 forced WWE to change WrestleMania 30's main event to include him. Currently AEW's highest-workrate performer.

## Career Highlights

- WWE Champion; AEW World Champion
- His ROH and early indie career built the template for modern American wrestling aesthetics
- The YES! Movement and WM30 main event win over HHH, Randy Orton, and Batista is the greatest fan-driven outcome in WWE history
- His technical match vs. Cesaro at NXT TakeOver (2015) is one of the best TV matches of the decade
- In AEW he has had canonical matches with Omega, Punk, Hangman, and MJF

## Why They Matter

Danielson's placement at #7 over legends like the Rock, Undertaker, and Cena reflects a specific values system: craft over charisma, ring work over character. He is the best argument that technical mastery and crowd connection are not mutually exclusive.
