# The Champ

**Real Name:** John Cena
**Born:** 1977, West Newbury MA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler / actor
**Active:** 2000–present
**Personal Rank:** #13 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#13. The most divisive star in WWE history — a man who sold out arenas to children while receiving sustained hate from adult male fans for a decade. 16-time world champion matching Ric Flair's record. His Never Give Up character and Make-A-Wish work (700+ wishes granted) reflects a genuine commitment to his role as a corporate ambassador. His current Hollywood career (Peacemaker) suggests the Rock trajectory is repeatable.

## Career Highlights

- 16x World Champion (joint WWE record with Ric Flair)
- His U.S. Open Challenge run (2015) produced match-of-the-year candidates weekly
- His WrestleMania matches with The Rock drew the two largest gates in WM history
- Make-A-Wish record holder among all celebrities
- Peacemaker (HBO Max) showed genuine comedic and dramatic range

## Why They Matter

Cena's career represents the WWE system working as designed: a physically imposing, charismatic, hard-working performer promoted to the top of the card and kept there for corporate reasons. His detractors were not wrong, but they also missed what made him great.
