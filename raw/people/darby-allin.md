# Darby Allin

**Real Name:** Samuel Burns
**Born:** 1992, Seattle WA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2015–present
**Personal Rank:** #49 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#49. The most physically committed performer in AEW — a man who takes bumps that professional wrestlers do not normally take and somehow survives them. His coffin drop (fall-away splash) is performed from heights that should injure him regularly. His skateboarding aesthetic and face paint give him a genuine punk rock credential. His feud with Samoa Joe (NXT 2020) and MJF (AEW 2022) are career highlights.

## Career Highlights

- AEW TNT Champion (multiple reigns); former AEW World Champion
- His matches with CM Punk in AEW have produced some of their respective best in-ring work
- The coffin drop — particularly off ladders, balconies, and structures — is wrestling's highest-risk regular spot
- His feud with Samoa Joe (NXT 2020) showed his range against a legitimately threatening opponent
- His physical commitment makes every match feel genuinely dangerous in a way that works without story

## Why They Matter

Darby's career could be shortened at any time by a bump that doesn't work out — that risk is part of what makes every match compelling. He has the most genuine 'punk' credibility of any professional wrestler.
