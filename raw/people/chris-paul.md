# Chris Paul

**Born:** 1985, Winston-Salem NC
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** PG
**Active:** 2005–present
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The most technically disciplined point guard in modern NBA history. One of the league's greatest floor generals — his ability to manage pace, pick his spots, and dictate the game's flow is unsurpassed. Twelve All-Star selections, four assists titles, one steal title. Reached the Finals with Phoenix (2021) and lost. Career defined by excellence without a championship.

## Career Highlights

- 12x All-Star; 4x assists champion; 1x steals champion
- His pick-and-roll chemistry with Chris Bosh, Dwight Howard, and Devin Booker shows his adaptability across eras
- His 2021 Suns playoff run (taking Phoenix to the Finals from 34-38 the previous season) is the best in-season turnaround in modern NBA history
- His court vision and pick-and-roll operation redefined what a point guard's primary skill set looks like
- He holds the record for most career assists while never winning a championship — the most unfortunate statistical legacy in NBA history
