# Gunther (WALTER)

**Real Name:** Walter Hahn
**Born:** 1988, Vienna, Austria
**Nationality:** Austrian
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2005–present
**Personal Rank:** #22 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#22. The most physically imposing legitimate technician in wrestling today. As WALTER in PROGRESS, wXw, and NJPW, he was the definitive European champion for six years before signing with WWE NXT UK. His NXT UK Championship run included matches with Pete Dunne and Ilja Dragunov that are among the best in recent wrestling history. His WWE Intercontinental Championship reign (2022–2023) is the longest since Honky Tonk Man's.

## Career Highlights

- NXT UK Champion; WWE Intercontinental Champion (longest modern reign); WWE World Champion
- His match with Ilja Dragunov at NXT UK TakeOver: Blackpool II (2020) is the best WWE match of that year — ★★★★¾
- His European championship run built a legacy before WWE signing that gave him genuine credibility
- The Gunther chop is the most feared offensive move in current wrestling — leaves legitimate welts
- His King of the Ring tournament win and subsequent world title run is the final validation

## Why They Matter

WALTER/Gunther is an Austrian heavyweight champion in an American industry built on American characters — the fact that he succeeded on pure physical credibility and ring work is the best argument for letting quality speak for itself.
