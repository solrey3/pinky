# Paul Pierce

**Born:** 1977, Oakland CA
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF
**Active:** 1998–2017
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

The Truth. One championship (2008 Celtics), Finals MVP, ten All-Star selections. Pierce's wheelchair incident and subsequent return in the 2008 Finals is basketball's most dramatic fake injury story. His clutch game-winners earned him the Truth nickname from Shaq, who coined it after Pierce made everything he threw at him.

## Career Highlights

- 1x NBA Champion (2008 Boston Celtics); 1x Finals MVP; 10x All-Star
- The Truth nickname given by Shaq after a 2001 regular season game — the highest compliment paid by one all-time great to another
- His 2008 Finals performance and the wheelchair incident defined the Celtics' championship identity
- His game 7 performance against LeBron (2012 ECF) is his finest individual hour
- A fundamentally sound scorer with the best midrange game of his era
