# James Worthy

**Born:** 1961, Gastonia NC
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF
**Active:** 1982–1994
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 1

## Summary

Big Game James. Three championships with the Showtime Lakers, one Finals MVP (1988). Worthy's Finals performances — particularly his 1988 Game 7 triple-double (36-16-10) — are the source of his nickname. The most complete third option on a dynasty team in NBA history.

## Career Highlights

- 3x NBA Champion (1985, 1987, 1988 Showtime Lakers); 1x Finals MVP
- His 1988 Finals Game 7 (36 pts, 16 reb, 10 ast triple-double) is the greatest triple-double in Finals history
- 7x All-Star; his baseline cuts and finishing around the basket were the model for wing scoring off movement
- His combination with Magic and Kareem is the most dominant three-man core in NBA history
- He is the standard for what 'third best player on a dynasty' looks like — completely reliable when it mattered
