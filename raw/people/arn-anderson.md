# Arn Anderson

**Real Name:** Martin Lunde
**Born:** 1958, Rome GA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1982–2001
**Personal Rank:** #69 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#69. The greatest tag team partner in wrestling history and the consummate 'enforcer' character. Arn Anderson's role in the Four Horsemen — the quiet muscle behind Ric Flair's flamboyance — is the template for faction architecture. His match quality was consistently excellent in a career that always operated in the shadow of bigger stars, and that's precisely what makes him respected.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple NWA/WCW US and TV Champion; Four Horsemen member
- The Double A spinebuster is the most imitated power move in wrestling — every major company has a version
- His role in the Four Horsemen defined the 'enforcer' archetype that all faction muscle characters since have modeled
- His feud with Barry Windham (1988) is the best pure wrestling feud in WCW/NWA of that era
- His WWE agent work (2019–present) influenced an entire developmental generation

## Why They Matter

Anderson is the definitive supporting cast member — a Hall of Famer who elevated everyone around him while rarely being the center of attention. His spinebuster is genuinely perfect.
