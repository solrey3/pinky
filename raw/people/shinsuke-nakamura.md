# The King of Strong Style

**Real Name:** Shinsuke Nakamura
**Born:** 1980, Kyoto, Japan
**Nationality:** Japanese
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2002–present
**Personal Rank:** #37 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#37. The most unique character in wrestling — his entrances are performance art pieces, his ring work blends shoot-style strikes with theatrical posing, and his charisma transcends the language barrier. His NXT career (2016–2017) produced one of the best first-year runs in NXT history. His match with Sami Zayn at NXT TakeOver Dallas is widely considered the greatest NXT match ever.

## Career Highlights

- IWGP Heavyweight Champion (NJPW); WWE IC Champion; NXT Champion
- His NXT TakeOver Dallas match with Sami Zayn (2016) is the best NXT match in history
- His King of Strong Style character and entrance theme ('Rising Sun') is uniquely affecting
- The Kinshasa running knee finish is one of the most visually perfect finishers in wrestling
- His work with Kota Ibushi in NJPW (2015) is some of the best heavyweight action of that year

## Why They Matter

Nakamura arrived in WWE already fully formed — a character with 15 years of NJPW credibility that didn't need the WWE developmental system. His inability to fully translate that in the main roster is WWE's failure, not his.
