# Sheamus

**Real Name:** Stephen Farrelly
**Born:** 1978, Dublin, Ireland
**Nationality:** Irish
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2002–present
**Personal Rank:** #64 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#64. The most physically convincing brawler in modern WWE. Sheamus's Brogue Kick is wrestling's most visually devastating kick finish, and his body — white as milk, covered in freckles, built like a tank — is entirely its own visual character. His tag team run with Cesaro (The Bar) is the most credible pure wrestling tag team of WWE's modern era. His IC title run (2015, 187 days) included great match after great match.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple WWE/US Champion; King of the Ring 2010; Money in the Bank
- The Bar tag team with Cesaro is the most consistently excellent pure wrestling tag team of 2010s WWE
- His Celtic Warrior character — built on genuine Irish physicality rather than an artificial gimmick — has natural credibility
- His IC title run (2015) produced weekly excellent matches that are under-discussed
- His matches with Daniel Bryan (Hell in a Cell 2012, WM28) are among the most underrated of that era

## Why They Matter

Sheamus is the wrestling equivalent of a player who was never the biggest star but always one of the better performers on the card. His commitment to the physical demands of the job is evident in every match.
