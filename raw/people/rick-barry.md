# Rick Barry

**Born:** 1944, Elizabeth NJ
**Nationality:** American
**Position:** SF
**Active:** 1965–1980
**Personal NBA Tier:** Level 2

## Summary

The greatest free throw shooter in NBA history (89.3% career on underhand 'granny' shots) and the first player to average 35+ ppg in the ABA. His underhanded free throw technique is objectively superior to overhand (statistically proven) and universally ignored by modern players. One NBA championship (1975 Warriors).

## Career Highlights

- 1x NBA Champion (1975 Golden State Warriors); ABA MVP 1969
- 89.3% career free throw percentage using underhanded technique — statistically the most efficient free throw method
- 35.0 ppg average in the ABA (1968–69) — the highest scoring average in ABA history
- His refusal to switch to overhand free throws, and modern players' refusal to switch to underhand, is basketball's greatest case study in ego over efficiency
- 8x NBA All-Star; 5x ABA All-Star
