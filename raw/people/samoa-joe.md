# Samoa Joe

**Real Name:** Nuufolau Joel Seanoa
**Born:** 1979, Huntington Beach CA
**Nationality:** Samoan-American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 1999–present
**Personal Rank:** #31 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#31. The most legitimately dangerous-seeming wrestler of his era. Joe's reputation was built on a TNA and ROH career that produced some of the best matches of the 2000s — his matches with CM Punk (ROH 2004) and AJ Styles (TNA 2005) are canonical. His NXT and WWE career validated everything his independent career had promised.

## Career Highlights

- NXT Champion; TNA World Champion
- His match with CM Punk at ROH Death Before Dishonor II (2004) is the best American wrestling match of that decade
- His TNA run (2005–2010) produced some of the best storytelling in TNA's history
- The Coquina Clutch is the most convincing submission finisher in wrestling — it looks genuinely suffocating
- His NXT debut and championship run (2015–2016) validated NXT as a legitimate wrestling brand

## Why They Matter

Joe's menace is entirely physical — the way he moves, the way he applies holds, the way he walks into an opponent's space — reads as genuinely dangerous. His technical ability was never the story; the story was always that you believed he would actually hurt you.
