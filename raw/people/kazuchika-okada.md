# The Rainmaker

**Real Name:** Kazuchika Okada
**Born:** 1988, Anjo, Japan
**Nationality:** Japanese
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2006–present
**Personal Rank:** #35 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#35. The greatest NJPW star of the post-Misawa era and arguably the most acclaimed title reign holder in the modern era. His IWGP championship matches — especially the Omega series — have produced more ★★★★★ matches than any other wrestler. His move to AEW in 2024 is the biggest NJPW departure in decades.

## Career Highlights

- Multiple IWGP Heavyweight Champion; AEW Continental Champion
- The Omega/Okada series (4 matches, 2017–2018) is the greatest sustained match series in wrestling history
- The Rainmaker lariat is the most visually impressive finishing move in modern wrestling
- His six-year IWGP title reign (non-consecutive but dominant) built NJPW into a globally watched product
- His AEW debut and championship run (2024) proved his value outside Japan

## Why They Matter

The Omega/Okada series received five stars from Meltzer for each of its four installments — something that had never happened before. Okada's contributions to that series are the argument that Japanese professional wrestling operates at a level of art that American wrestling has not matched.
