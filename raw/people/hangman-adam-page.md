# Hangman Adam Page

**Real Name:** Adam Scherr
**Born:** 1991, Hickory NC
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Professional wrestler
**Active:** 2013–present
**Personal Rank:** #19 (Pro Wrestling All-Time)

## Summary

#19. The most emotionally resonant babyface arc in AEW history — a man who beat Kenny Omega for the AEW World Championship after two years of failure, self-doubt, and alcohol as a storyline coping mechanism. His match with Kenny Omega at Full Gear 2021 is among the best matches in AEW history. The 'Anxious Millennial Cowboy' character is a genuinely new wrestling archetype.

## Career Highlights

- AEW World Champion (Full Gear 2021)
- His two-year title chase — from almost winning at AEW All Out 2019 to winning at Full Gear 2021 — is the best long-term babyface booking in modern wrestling
- His matches with Kenny Omega and Bryan Danielson are both among the best of their respective years
- The cowboy character (flannel, beer, horse) works as both comedy and genuine emotional shorthand
- His promo work — quiet, sincere, conflicted — is the antithesis of the wrestling promo cliché

## Why They Matter

Page's championship win was the rare wrestling moment that felt genuinely earned because the audience had invested in his failures. The slow-build character arc required trust from AEW that paid off in one of the most emotionally complete wrestling matches in recent memory.
