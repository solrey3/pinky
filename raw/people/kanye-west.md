# Kanye West

**Born:** 1977, Atlanta GA
**Nationality:** American
**Profession:** Rapper, producer, designer
**Active:** 2003–present

## Summary

The most innovative hip-hop producer of his generation and the artist who most consistently expanded what rap albums could be. The College Dropout through My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy (2004–2010) is the most commercially successful and critically acclaimed run in hip-hop production history. His personal conduct and antisemitism since 2022 have complicated a legacy that was previously the strongest in hip-hop.

## Key Works / Highlights

- My Beautiful Dark Twisted Fantasy (2010) — widely considered the best hip-hop album of the 2010s; a Metacritic 94
- The College Dropout (2004) — #39 personal ranking; launched his production style and alternative rap persona
- Graduation (2007) — ended the 50 Cent vs. Kanye sales battle (527K vs. 691K first week) and signaled hip-hop's mainstream transition to sampling-free production
- His production work for Jay-Z (Blueprint), Alicia Keys, Talib Kweli, Mos Def established his pre-rapper credentials
- His fashion career (Yeezy) is the most commercially successful musician-to-fashion transition in history

## Why They Matter

West's artistic legacy is genuinely complex in 2026 — his musical output through MBDTF is the strongest run in modern hip-hop production; his personal conduct since 2022 is documented and indefensible. Both are true simultaneously. His contribution to how hip-hop sounds is total and permanent.
